Justin Bieber Completes Sleeve Tattoo, Says He's Done For Now

The last Bieber tattoo! For right now, at least. Troubled pop star Justin Bieber took to Instagram on March 25 to share his fresh ink with his fans. The “Boyfriend” singer, 20, who already has a slew of tattoos covering his body, completed his second sleeve on his right arm. 

“Love!!!! @seunghunjotattoos great job man,” the Biebs captioned one picture with Toronto-based tattoo artist Seunghyun Jo. 

The new sleeve features a variety of drawings surrounding two slightly older tattoos he received over two months ago of a jester’s head and the word “Love” written in Old English script. Adding on to those two tattoos is a smiling Korean mask, a boom box  and the word “Trust” in big letters. 

“I love you Korea,” Bieber captioned the photo of the inside of his arm. 

On the outside of the sleeve are drawings of a diamond, a black spade, an 8 ball, an angry version of the Kirby cartoon, and a depiction of graffiti artist Banksy’s famous picture of a little girl reaching out towards a heart balloon.

Now that this “masterpiece” is complete, the Grammy nominee claims to be done with body art.
