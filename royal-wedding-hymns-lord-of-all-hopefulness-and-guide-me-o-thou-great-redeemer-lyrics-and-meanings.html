Royal Wedding hymns - Lord Of All Hopefulness and Guide Me, O Thou Great Redeemer lyrics

PRINCE Harry's wedding to Meghan Markle will include some classic hymns.

Here's everything you need to know about Lord Of All Hopefulness and Guide Me, O Thou Great Redeemer which will be sung at the ceremony...

Who is Jan Struther who wrote Lord Of All Hopefulness?

Jan Struther was the pen name of Joyce Anstruther born in 1901 in Buckinghamshire.

She was a writer and best known for her character Mrs Miniver and several hymns, including Lord Of All Hopefulness.

Jan Struther was also the great-aunt of Ian Maxtone Graham, former co-executive producer of The Simpsons.

She died of breast cancer in New York in 1953 at the age of 52.

What are the lyrics to Lord Of All Hopefulness?

Lord of all hopefulness,
Lord of all joy,
Whose trust, ever child-like,
No cares could destroy,
Be there at our waking,
And give us, we pray,
Your bliss in our hearts, Lord,
At the break of the day.

Lord of all eagerness, Lord of all faith,
Whose strong hands were skilled
At the plane and the lathe,
Be there at our labours,
And give us, we pray,
Your strength in our hearts, Lord,
At the noon of the day.

Lord of all kindliness, Lord of all grace,
Your hands swift to welcome,
Your arms to embrace,
Be there at our homing,
And give us, we pray,
Your love in our hearts, Lord,
At the eve of the day.

Lord of all gentleness, Lord of all calm,
Whose voice is contentment,
Whose presence is balm,
Be there at our sleeping,
And give us, we pray,
Your peace in our hearts, Lord,
At the end of the day.

Who is John Hughes who wrote Guide Me, O Thou Great Redeemer?

John Hughes was a Welsh composer who lived from 1873-1932.

He grew up in  Llanilltud Faerdref and served as a deacon and leader of the congregational singing in Salem Baptist Chapel.

Guide Me, O Thou Great Redeemer (Cwm Rhondda in Welsh) was written in 1907 and the lyrics are taken from the William Williams' text.

He also penned the famous tune Cwm Rhondda, taken from the Welsh name for the Rhondda Valley.
