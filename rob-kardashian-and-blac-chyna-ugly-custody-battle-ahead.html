Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna: Ugly Custody Battle Ahead!

In an especially moving and vulnerable recent Instagram post, Rob Kardashian said goodbye to his daughter.

"He wasn’t leaving little Dream for an extended period of time, but he still shared a photo of the child online and wrote as a caption:

"Saying bye to my beautiful baby girl … she is smiling at me. U see how she looking at her daddy. I literally can’t get enough of this girl."

Tragically, however, a new report makes it sound as if Rob may need to get used to seeing just barely enough of this little girl.

The sock designer split from his body-flaunting girlfriend, Blac Chyna, several weeks ago.

For a couple that fought as often as it made love, the final break-up was actually quite amicable, with sources saying Chyna and Rob wanted to handle things in a "mature" manner for a change.

That mission has been accomplished.

But an insider tells People Magazine that the biggest fight of all-time between the reality stars may soon take place; one centered around the future of their daughter.

“I expect things to get ugly,” this source explains. “Chyna wants full custody with Rob only getting visitations.”

Rob and Chyna were engaged, but never got married.

A previous Radar Online report echoed this allegation, as someone close to the situation gave that celebrity gossip website the same scoop.

"Dream is her child and she wants to have her all of the time. She can have supervised visits with Rob, but Chyna is the main parent," Radar wrote just a few days ago.

This report claimed that Kris Jenner would battle like heck against Chyna’s full custody request, but there’s only so much Kris Jenner could actually do in this circumstance.

Throughout the duration of her romance with Rob, members of his famous family have taken issue with Chyna.

"But they were happy when the relationship was working and when Rob was happy,” this People insider says, adding that tension has still remained since the very beginning:

"At the end of the day, they all raise an eyebrow at Chyna and her intentions.”

Rob has been through a great deal himself.

He’s struggled with severe weight gain as a result of his Diabetes diagnosis at times and he’s reportedly had problems with drinking or drug use in the past.

Rob is currently filming new episodes of Keeping Up with the Kardashians.

Production is “continuing to shoot with all the family members, and are covering Rob and his relationship with Chyna," People writes.

This unexpected relationship became public knowledge a little over a year ago, in January of 2016.

It initially raised many, many eyebrows due to Chyna’s complex history with the Kardashian-Jenner clan: she shares a younh son with Kylie Jenner‘s series boyfriend Tyga… and is also close friends with Amber Rose, an ex-girlfriend of Kanye West.

Rob proposed to Chyna in April of 2016.

One month later, the stars announced they were expecting their first child; they welcomed Dream in November.
