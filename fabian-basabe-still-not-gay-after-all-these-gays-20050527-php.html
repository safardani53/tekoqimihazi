Fabian Basabe: Still not gay after all these gays — Fri, May 27, 2005 —

The photo below is a visual definition of why publicists are necessary evils: To protect you from photos like the photo below from ever surfacing while you fight to keep your sexuality in the closet and your marriage from becoming an admitted sham.

And it’s photos like the photo below that surface just in time to coincide with a our Memorial Day Weekend send-off, where we say we’ll totally be at your party in Sag Harbor when Fire Island sounds like a more likely destination. (Oh, and don’t expect to hear from us till Tuesday.)

So here you have it: Fabian Basabe playing it straight will a couple shirtless muscle guys with their tanks painted on.

He was at Animal Fair magazine’s Paws for Style party at Exit – and yes, his wife Martina Borgomanero Basabe was with him, though it appears a photog-swatting publicist was not.

Happy Memorial Hamptons Season Day Weekend!
