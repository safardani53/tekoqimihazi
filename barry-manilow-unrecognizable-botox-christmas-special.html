Barry Manilow, 80, looks unrecognizable as fans say he 'can't open his mouth after too much botox

BARRY Manilow has fans claiming he looks unrecognizable after taking his rumored plastic surgery procedures too far.

The singer's appearance has changed drastically through the years as some fans claim he can hardly open his mouth due to his apparent cosmetic work.

Barry, 80, took the stage for an NBC Christmas special on Monday, performing some festive hits during the show.

The iconic singer wowed longtime fans but left many viewers focused on something other than his singing.

Many took to X, formerly Twitter, to weigh in on the award-winning musician's appearance.

Viewers plead with Barry to slow down on the Botox.

BARRY CHRISTMAS

Barry took the stage for an NBC special called A Very Barry Christmas.

The show marked the singer's third Christmas-themed TV special.

The one-hour show was filmed at the Westgate Las Vegas Resort & Casino, which is the same venue at which the musician played his 637th show.

The special featured classic Christmas songs as well as some of Barry's better-known hits.

"It's filled with great Christmas songs. I've had three Christmas albums out, so I could have gone on for hours because they're wonderful," the singer said ahead of the show.

"I actually figured out a way to put my own pop songs into the Christmas show, so even though I'm singing Copacabana, it always feels like a Christmas show."

LET'S FACE IT

Rumors have been swirling about Barry's appearance for years.

In 2019, fans noticed that the star appeared expressionless as he left his Broadway show.

While he was reported to have been struggling in his personal life at the time it was reported that his face was "oddly still" as he chatted with fans.

Rumors swirled at the time that he and manager-husband Garry Kief were "fighting horribly."

The reports came amid ticket sales struggles, of which a source claimed: "If he weren't his husband, Barry would have fired Garry by now."

The pair are still married, however.
