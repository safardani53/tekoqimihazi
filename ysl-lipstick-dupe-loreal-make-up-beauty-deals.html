Beauty fan shows off dupe to her fave YSL lipstick - it smells like old lady make-up but for a

A MAKE-UP whizz has revealed the ultimate dupe for a cult-loved Yves Saint Laurent Beauty lipstick. 

The YSL Rouge Volupté Shine Lipstick in shade 44 is famous for its moisturising formula. 

But at £35 a pop, it's not an affordable option for everyone. 

Luckily, beauty content creator Nicole (@nikkmatt) has shared the high-street dupe for it. 

The L'Oreal Paris Colour Riche Satin Lipstick 800 is a dead ringer for the designer version and only costs £7.99 in Boots. 

In the UK, the L’Oreal shade name to look out for is Fairest Nude.

The price difference means you’ll save a whopping £27 if you choose to get your dupe on. 

Nicole shared a review and side-by-side comparison of the two lipsticks in a TikTok video. 

“Let’s see if this is a dupe for one of my favourite lipsticks by YSL,” she said.

“This is YSL’s shade number 44: it’s a beautiful pinky, peachy colour. Very shiny, very moisturising. 

Most read in Fabulous

“It’s an everyday nude lipstick.”

Swatching the YSL and L’Oreal lipsticks on the back of her hand, Nicole “noticed some differences right off the bat”.

“The L’Oreal one is definitely a little more peachy,” she said. 

“But let’s see it on the lips.”

Nicole applied the two lipsticks on opposite sides of her mouth to see how close in colour they really were - and she was seriously surprised.

Despite the colour differences when swatched on her hands, the difference in shades was “undetectable on the lips”.

“This is such a dupe,” she added.

Nicole’s only qualm with L’Oreal lipstick is that it has an “old lady” and “grandma” smell. 

She noted this after one woman remarked: “I’ve never liked the scent of L’Oréal lipstick”.

But for a £27 price difference, who cares if your lips smell like an “old lady’s”?
