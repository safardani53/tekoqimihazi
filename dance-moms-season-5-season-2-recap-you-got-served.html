Dance Moms Season 5 Season 2 Recap: You Got Served

Last night on Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 2, Abby Lee Miller got served, and HARD. You could say this was a dish best served ice cold … or something.

What on Earth are we even talking about, you ask?

Watch Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 2 Online

Picking up where last week’s premiere left off, Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 2 set up the Maddie vs. Kalani showdown that the world has been waiting for.

“This is the big showdown. Everybody has been waiting for it,” Abby said of Maddie Ziegler, 12, and Kalani Hilliker, 14, preparing to dance for supremacy.

Abby has more on her plate than this Clash of Pint Sized Titans, though.

Miller’s rivals were gunning for her like never before, and not just on the dance floor, as she was slapped with court documents by ex-student Paige Hyland, 14.

If you watch Dance Moms online, you know the ugly history there.

For the most part, this installment focused on the Abby Lee Dance Company and Kalani, who was previously denied the chance to go against Maddie.

So why the change of heart? A solo appearance by her former ensemble team member Ava Cota, 12, and her mom, Jeanette, may have been a factor.

The team traveled to Detroit to be confronted by Jeanette’s team, JC’s Broadway Dance Academy, and Jeanette filed an official complaint against Abby.

For allegedly getting all up in one of her girls’ grills. Obviously.

Abby denied all allegations as always in an epic argument, after which the ALDC team managed to win first place for their Orange is the New Black number.

The soloists’ fortunes were not quite as rosy last night.

Maddie managed to score second place, but Kalani only took fourth, and adding insult to injury, she was bested by Ava’s piece, which placed third.

Abby does not take losing well, as we all know.

Angrily calling the whole competition “rigged” following the awards ceremony, Abby was criticized by dance mom Holly Frazier, and responded in kind.

Abby told Holly her daughter Nia Frazier, 14, has “pathetic feet” and you know, she doesn’t need to follow her to her West Coast studio, ALDC LA. Burrrrrrrn.

Miller also ripped Kendall Vertes, 12, for failing to introduce herself during an impromptu audition exercise. and calling her mom Jill a “bad mother.”

“Kendall, your mom will stick her hand up your butt and move your mouth like a puppet,” Abby told Kendall, who begged her coach to stop, to no avail.

“Quit disrespecting everybody!” Kendall’s friend Nia said to Abby.

“OK. But you’re not respecting me,” Abby Lee Miller replied.

Is the team coming apart at the seams? Stay tuned.
