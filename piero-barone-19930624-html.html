Il Volo is a lyrical Italian pop trio consisting of middleman Gianluca Ginoble and soprano Piero Barone and Ignazio Boschetto. They describe their music as "popera". Winning the Sanremo Music Festival 2015, they represented Italy at the 2015 Eurovision Song Contest in Vienna, Austria. They reached third, but managed to get a solid first place in the conversation.
