28 Motivating Walter Payton Quotes

Walter Payton is an American football legend who was born in 1954 and died in 1999. He has earned an incredible place in history through his career rushing record of 16,726 yards.

He is also regarded as an all-time high in the National Football League. His entire 13-year career was spent playing with the Chicago Bears. He loves to compete and always intended to win.

Moreover, he is remembered today as a prolific rusher who had to hold records for career rushing yards, touchdowns, all-purpose yards, and many more.

His versatility made him earn the reputation and fans which his soul has today. In 1993, he was elected into the College Football Hall of Fame. There he was described as the greatest player by the players inside the field and coach Mike Ditka.

His saying and quotes are also famous as his personality, and some of them are listed below.
