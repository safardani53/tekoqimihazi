I found a $9 drugstore dupe for the viral $40 Dior lip oil - it's 'super juicy' and you can't tell t

A BEAUTY guru has found an affordable dupe for a pricey Dior product.

For every new beauty product that comes out, there is a dupe on the way.

These affordable versions of high-end products allow people to stay on trend without breaking the bank.

Currently, one of the biggest beauty trends is lip oil, a non-sticky alternative to lip gloss.

Dior's Addict Lip Glow Oil is one of the hottest products that has come from the trend.

It is offered in natural and shiny finishes in eight shades.

read more on dupes

A single tube of the lip oil costs an eyewatering $40.

Luckily, TikToker Kelly Strack (@kellystrackofficial) found a dupe that costs just $7.

In one of her most recent videos, she admitted that while the Dior product was beautiful, the price was "super steep for a lip oil."

She suggested the Nyx Fat Oil Lip Drip as a dupe, which retails for just $9.

Most read in Money

"When you put them next to each other on the lips you can barely tell any difference at all," she said.

"It gives a super juicy, glossy look to the lips. Definitely a dupe."

The Nyx lip oil also comes in eight shades and boasts that it offers "12 hours of hydration and protection."

MONEY-SAVING FINDS

Another TikToker found a dupe at Walmart for the cult-classic Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk lipstick.

Despite going for $35 a pop, a tube of the lipstick reportedly sells every two minutes.

"Have you ever looked at the price of your favorite makeup product and wished it was more affordable?" makeup expert (@krity_s) said in their video.

The beauty pro suggested picking up the L'Oreal Colour Riche Les Nus Intense Lipstick in the shade Nu Impertinent.

When put next to each other, the lip colors look identical.

"It's the exact same dupe at a fraction of the cost," she said. "Lasts just as long and has a great texture."

The best part is that the L'Oreal lipstick costs just $7.
