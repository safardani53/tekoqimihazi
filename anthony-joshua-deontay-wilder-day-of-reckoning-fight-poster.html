Boxing fans say 'this goes hard' after 'fire' Day of Reckoning poster with Anthony Joshua and Deonta

THE OFFICIAL fight poster for boxing's Day of Reckoning has been released - and fans are loving it.

We are now just over two weeks ago from what promises to be a monster night of fights in Saudi Arabia.

Anthony Joshua will be hoping to continue his rise back to the top on December 23 when he faces Otto Wallin in Riyadh.

But the Brit is sharing the main event billing with fellow heavyweight superstar Deontay Wilder.

The former WBC champion will be in action against another former heavyweight title holder, Joseph Parker.

Almost the entire boxing world is hoping that both AJ and the Bronze Bomber can pull off victories in their respective bouts and then go on to finally agree a long-awaited fight between them.

The card is stacked going to be a massive one, packed with heavyweight bust-ups, and Daniel Dubois is also going to be in action.

Dubois will take on former drug cheat Jarrell Miller, who was supposed to fight Joshua back in June 2019.
