Who is Shawnna:

Shawnna is a famous Rapper. She was born on January 3, 1978 and her birthplace is Chicago, IL. Shawnna is also well known as, Rapper who first found fame as a member of the duo Infamous Syndicate in the late 90’s before making her solo debut with the 2002 album Worth tha Weight. She is known to fans for her speedy delivery and explicit lyrical themes as heard on songs like “Gettin’ Some.”

Shawnna is originated from United States. She was featured on Ludacris’ “Stand Up,” making her one of the few female rappers ever to be featured on a song to top the Billboard singles charts.
