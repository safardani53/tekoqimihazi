Morgan Wade No Signs Of Slowing Down tour: How can I buy tickets?

COUNTRY music artist Morgan Wade announced she is going on her first tour across the US.

Wade will introduce a variety of opening acts and will be joined by singer Kaitlin Butts for the majority of the tour.

When is Morgan Wade's tour?

Morgan Wade is setting off on her No Signs of Slowing Down tour on February 24, 2023.

Her tour will kick off in Louisville, Kentucky, and will continue through April 16 when she will end the tour in her home state in Richmond, Virginia.

The new tour comes after she dropped her first album, Reckless, in March 2021 and released her Acoustic Sessions EP featuring three new songs in August 2022.

Wade announced the tour on her Instagram, writing: “Been a long time coming — I’m finally going to headline my first tour.

"I’m excited to show y’all what the boys and I can do. Most of all thank you to all the fans that make something like this possible for me to do."

Wade also said she will be joined by Kaitlin Butts and other rotating opening acts including Kyle Kelly, Nate Fredrick, and Megan McRee.

Speaking about the upcoming tour, Wade told Pollstar: “I’ve been going hard since Reckless came out. And we’re not stopping.

"If people want to hear this music, we’re gonna bring it, but now we’re gonna bring it all."

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets to the No Signs of Slowing Down tour go on sale September 30, 2022, at 10am local time.

There will be presale tickets available on September 28, 2022, at 10am local time.

Those looking to purchase presale or regular tickets can do so on Ticketmaster, VividSeats, and Songkick.
