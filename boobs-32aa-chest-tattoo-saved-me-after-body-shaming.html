I have 34AA boobs - my chest tattoo 'saved me' after being body shamed over their size, now my look

A WOMAN has shared her experience of being body shamed over her small boobs, as well as how she overcame her insecurities in order to empower others.

The personal trainer previously explained that her small chest was a big insecurity for her "back in the day" but is no longer a problem for her.

In the video Cait wears a low-cut halterneck top and blue jeans and she starts off with her arms covering her chest.
