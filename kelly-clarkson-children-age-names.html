How many children does Kelly Clarkson have?

KELLY Clarkson was barely past her teenage years when she competed in American Idol.

Flash forward years later, the GRAMMY Award winner now has children of her own.

How many children does Kelly Clarkson have?

On October 20, 2013, Kelly Clarkson and Brandon Blackstock tied the knot at Blackberry Farms in Tennessee.

On June 12, 2014, the pair welcomed their first child together.

Kelly's daughter, River Rose, is the first of her two children and the third of Brandon's total of four kids.

On April 12, 2016, the couple expanded their family even further with the arrival of their second child together.

Read More on Kelly's kids

Six years following their son Remington's birth, Kelly and Brandon hit splitsville and their divorce was finalized on March 8, 2022.

Regarding her life with her daughter River, Kelly told PEOPLE: "I love that River is very bold, she’s progressive for three.

"I don’t want my daughter to be a pushover when she’s older. So that’s an awesome thing, it’s an awesome aspect of her character. At the same time, you also want to have an adult that doesn’t think they’re right all the time."

The Voice coach added: "She will run a company one day because there’s no way she’s gonna work for anyone. She does not heed advice very well. She’s just very ballsy, which is awesome."

While Kelly describes River as "bold" and "progressive," the talk show host describes her son Remington as "a sensitive soul."

How many stepchildren does Kelly have?

Through her former marriage to ex-husband Brandon, Kelly has two stepchildren.

Kelly's stepchildren Savannah and Seth are a product of Brandon's first marriage to Melissa Ashworth White.

Despite the divorce between Kelly and Brandon, the former remains close to Savannah and Seth.

An insider revealed: "She's always thought of Seth and Savannah as her own too, and she has a special relationship with both of them.

"Kelly wants to remain close with her step-kids. Her priority is making sure her children grow up in a stable, loving environment."
