Madonna's shock as she's BANNED from Instagram after posting naked pictures

MADONNA was left gobsmacked when she discovered she has been banned from going live on Instagram after posting a series of naked pictures.

The 63-year-old was about to chat to her fans on the platform when a notification came up saying she was blocked, leaving her exclaiming: “What the f**k.”

Angry Madonna continued: "We're blocked from live? What's happening? I've never worn so many clothes in my life. I'm speechless."

Sharing the clip online, a furious Madonna captioned it: "Not Us!!!! violation community guidelines………."

In the past Madonna has images deleted by Instagram because they are against the guidelines.

Last year the mum-of-six posted graphic snaps of her nipples and her butt in bondage-style lingerie.
