Golden Bachelor finalist Faith Martin sparks concern as she unveils bandaged face and reveals scary

FAITH Martin has given fans a warning after revealing she was diagnosed with basal cell carcinoma.

The Golden Bachelor contestant was seen with a bandage around her nose as she spoke to followers about the importance of wearing sunscreen.

While going on Instagram Live, Faith, 60, sat next to her son in a booth at IHOP as she gave an update on her current health situation.

"This is why you need to wear sunscreen," Faith said as she pointed to the white bandage covering most of her nose.

Faith, who was sent home by Gerry Turner, 72, after the season's hometown dates, revealed she was diagnosed with basal cell carcinoma.

She explained that she had the first part of the procedure done to remove the cancer, and now a plastic surgeon will go back in to close the hole.

She said she doesn't know how long she will have to wear the bandage, joking: "You think nobody's gonna want to date me like this?"
