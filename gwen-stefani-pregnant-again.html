Gwen Stefani: Pregnant Again!

Numerous sources are reporting that Gwen Stefani is 13 weeks pregnant with her second child. That’s great news for her and husband Gavin Rossdale.

It’s potentially bad news for the forthcoming baby, however, as he/she will have trouble competing with the sheer cuteness of brother Kingston. Nevertheless, a source said:

“[Gwen and Gavin] found out at California’s Cedars-Sinai hospital and couldn’t wait to share the news with everyone.”

Congratulations to Gwen! The rocker joins a handful of other celebrities that are knocked up, including: Jamie Lynn Spears, Camila Alves, Jessica Alba, Halle Berry, Jennifer Lopez and possibly Angelina Jolie.
