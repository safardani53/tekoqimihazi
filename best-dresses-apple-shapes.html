What is an apple shape?

If you're an apple, you'll typically have a larger bust and fuller midsection, along with slender legs and narrow hips. In clothing terms, this means you may look for dresses that add definition to your waistline, while showing off your favourite features like your legs.

What style dress looks best on apple shape?

The first step to finding your perfect outfit is figuring out your body type. Apple and pear shapes may seem similar, but there are a few key differences.

Apple shapes will tend to carry their weight around their middle, with broad shoulders and a big bust, but in contrast, their arms and legs will be slender.

Pear shapes hold more weight at their hips instead of the waist and tend to be smaller up top.

Can apple shapes wear jumpsuits?

Yes, absolutely! The key to dressing for all shapes is if it makes you feel amazing: wear it. For a more defined waistline, try a jumpsuit that nips in at the waist rather than drapes over it.

Are wrap dresses good for apple shapes?

Apple shapes love a defined waist, and a wrap dress is a perfect way to flatter your figure.
