Boobs and Blunts Galore in Topless Instagram Photo!

It’s not often that we get to combine all of our favorite things in one photo, but that’s just what Robyn Rihanna Fenty managed to do with her latest Intstagram pic:

Yes, as you may already know, Rihanna likes to get naked. She also really, really likes to smoke weed. 

So naturally, she figured she’d gather some of her closest friends take her top off, spark a blunt, and pose for a weird Addams Family pic.

We kid. That’s actually RiRi backstage at the The Vineyard in Beverly Hills, where she hosted her first annual Diamond Ball over the weekend.

The event raised money for the Clara Lionel Foundation – a charitable organization that Rihanna founded in 2012 and named after her grandparents.

The list of A-listers in attendance is too long to reproduce here, but sources say Brad Pitt stole the show with a sarcastic speech that gradually took on the tone of a Friar’s Club roast:

“I ask that we stand together and show her our support and love and say to her the hard words,” Brad said of the guest of honor. “Rihanna, put down that sandwich, get off your ass and do something. Just do something with your life!”

We’re sure RiRi appreciated the joke…unless she was so baked that she just zoned out mid-speech.

In related news: none of the guests were able to provide any new information as to whether or not Rihanna is dating Steven Klein, as her new “friend” was reportedly not in attendance.

But again, she could’ve just been so stoned that she forgot to invite him.
