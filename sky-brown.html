Who is Sky Brown:

Sky Brown is a famous Skateboarder. She was born on July 12, 2008 and her birthplace is Miyazaki, Japan. Sky is also well known as, Skateboarder who became the youngest girl ever to compete in the 2016 Vans US Open Pro Series. She’d later become the winner of the inaugural season of Dancing with the Stars: Juniors in 2018.

Sky is originated from Japan. She was paired with JT Church on DWTS Juniors.
