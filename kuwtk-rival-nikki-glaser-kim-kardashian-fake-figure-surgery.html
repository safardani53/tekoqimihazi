KUWTK rival Nikki Glaser RIPS Kim Kardashian for 'fake' figure &amp; insists her body is 'from surgery'

KEEPING Up With The Kardashians rival Nikki Glaser has ripped Kim Kardashian for her "fake" figure and insisted her body was made "from surgery."

Nikki, 38, slammed the most recent Instagram post by Kim, 41. 

In the sexy snapshot, the KUWTK alum showed off her bikini body. 

The SKIMS founder modeled in shiny and metallic swimwear.

The Kardashians star had on reflective sunglasses as she tied her blonde hair into a bun. 

She took the mirror selfie while posing in the middle of the bathroom.

NIKKI'S BILLBOARD

This is not the first time the Not Safe with Nikki Glaser star has had something to say about the famous reality star.

In May, the comic threw shade at the Hulu star with a billboard.  

Fans spotted a hilarious billboard that was promoting Nikki's new series.

The caption appeared to poke fun at the Kardashian family's switch from E! to the streaming service.

The billboard also featured Nikki standing in front of the giant E! logo.

The tagline: "Keeping up is so last year" was displayed along with her.

"Welcome Home Nikki Glaser? A New Reality Series" was also included underneath the caption.

Before signing their lucrative deal with Hulu, the Kardashians had a nearly 15-year run on the E! network and a hit show that was executive produced by Ryan Seacrest, 47.

In September 2020, Kim confirmed the move to Hulu and revealed that her other TV series was ending.

Nikki's E! show - which followed her as she moved back to St. Louis with her parents - premiered in May.
