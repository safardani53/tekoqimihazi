Rachel Levin Biography

Rachel Levin's full name Rachel C. Levin is a YouTuber from the United States. Levin is popular on YouTube for her own where she uploads beauty tips. She is also a Video blogger.

Rachel Levin was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, the United States of America. Her mother Debra Levin is a lawyer and her father is a doctor in Pennsylvania. She is an American and is of white ethnicity. She has a sister named Daniella Levin. Levin began her college studies at Pennsylvania State University.

Levin, since the age of 15 started uploading videos of makeup routines onto her YouTube channel . Her channel got huge fame and now she has 13.5 million subscribers as of July 5, 2018, with her total views of 3 billion as of April 8, 2018.

On December 15, 2010, Levin published her first video named "How to Conceal Under Eye Dark Circles" on her channel. Also in December 2010, she began Vlogging. Her videos feature
beauty, fashion, lifestyle, and comedy as well.

Talking about her personal life, Rachel is not married yet, but she does date her boyfriend. Currently, she is in a relationship with Tyler Regan, whom she calls Bambi. The couple started dating in May 2017 and are still together without any mishaps of separation.

Prior to Regan, Rachel Levin was in a relationship with her former boyfriend named Issac Nakash, however, after dating for 3 years, the duos broke up in November 2016. Rachel has over 3.7 million followers on Instagram and 3.61 million followers on Twitter.

Rachel, 23, along with huge fame is also enjoying a huge net worth. As of 2018, her net worth is estimated to be $3 million.
