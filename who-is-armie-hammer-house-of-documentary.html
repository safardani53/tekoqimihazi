Who is Armie Hammer? | The US Sun

FAMED actor Armie Hammer rose to prominence after starring in a slew of popular movies.

After Hammer achieved his breakout role in The Social Network, he went on to appear in films such as Call Me By Your Name and Death on the Nile.

Who is Armie Hammer?

Born on August 28, 1986, Armie Hammer is an American actor from Santa Monica, California.

Before his mainstream stardom, Armie appeared in small recurring roles on a slew of television shows.

His appearances include Desperate Housewives, Gossip Girl, Arrested Development, and Veronica Mars.

In 2010, Hammer starred alongside Jesse Eisenberg, Justin Timberlake, and Andrew Garfield in the biographical drama film The Social Network.

He continued to earn recognition by accumulating motion picture credits for The Man From U.N.C.L.E, The Lone Ranger, J. Edgar, and Mirror, Mirror.

The 2017 romantic drama flick, Call Me By Your Name, saw Hammer star opposite Timothée Chalamet as the two shared screen time as love interests.

Is Armie Hammer married and does he have kids?

On May 22, 2010, Armie Hammer tied the knot with TV personality and celebrity chef Elizabeth Chambers in a California wedding ceremony.

Armie and Elizabeth have two children together, a daughter named Harper and a son named Ford.

On July 10, 2020, the couple announced they were separating through a post on social media.

On Instagram, they released a joint statement that said: "Thirteen years as best friends, soulmates, partners, and then parents."

"It has been an incredible journey, but together, we’ve decided to turn the page and move on from our marriage."
