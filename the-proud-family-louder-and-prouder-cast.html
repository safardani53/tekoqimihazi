The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder: Who is in the cast?

ORIGINALLY on Disney Channel from 2001 until 2005, The Proud Family was a popular animated series.

On Wednesday, February 23, 2022, the spin-off known as The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder premiered on Disney+.

Who is in the cast?

The Proud Family

Kyla Pratt as Penny Proud

Born on September 16, 1986, Kyla Pratt is an actress.

She appeared as a main character within the Dr. Dolittle franchise and has starred in films such as Fat Albert and Hotel for Dogs.

Pratt also worked on shows such as Let's Stay Together, Black Ink Crew: Compton, and Call Me Kat.

Jo Marie Payton as Suga Mama

The beloved Suga Mama is voiced by Jo Marie Payton, an actress and singer.

Born on August 3, 1950, Payton has appeared in shows such as Family Matters and Perfect Strangers.

Paula Jai Parker as Trudy Proud

Born on August 19, 1969, Paula Jai Parker is an actress, comedian, director, and musician.

Parker has starred in films such as Friday, Sprung, Why Do Fools Fall in Love, Phone Booth, Hustle & Flow, and Idlewild.

She is also known for her role as Monique on The Wayans Bros.

Tommy Davidson as Oscar Proud

Born on November 10, 1963, Tommy Davidson is an actor and comedian.

An original cast member of the sketch comedy show In Living Color, Davidson is also known for his roles in projects such as Malcolm and Eddie, Bamboozled, and Black Dynamite.

Cedric the Entertainer as Uncle Bobby

Born on April 24, 1964, Cedric the Entertainer is an actor, stand-up comedian, and television personality.

Known for his work on Def Comedy Jam and The Steve Harvey Show, Cedric went on to briefly host Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?.

He received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2019.

Friends and Neighbors

Karen Malina White as Dijonay Jones

Born on July 7, 1965, Karen Malina White is an actress known for her work across both film and television.

She appeared as Kaneesha Carter in the 1989 film Lean on Me, and starred as Charmaine Brown on The Cosby Show and its spin-off: A Different World.

Soleil Moon Frye as Zoey Howzer

Born on August 6, 1976, Soleil Moon Frye is an actress, director, screenwriter, and producer.

She's been active in the entertainment industry since the age of two and went on to star in the titular role of Punky Brewster in the NBC sitcom.

Alisa Reyes as LaCienega Boulevardez

Alisa Reyes is an actress and model known mostly for her lead role on the sketch comedy show, All That.
