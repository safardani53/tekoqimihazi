Kylie Jenner flashes her legs in a white mini dress and purple boots at fashion launch party

KYLIE Jenner gave a glimpse of her svelte legs as she rocked an eye-catching pair of purple thigh-high boots at a fashion event in Los Angeles.

All eyes were very much on the 19-year-old's vibrant choice of footwear which she teamed with a white floaty minidress at the PrettyLittleThing campaign launch on Tuesday night.

The beauty's long raven hair cascaded down her front, while her make-up was flawlessly applied.

As her minder accompanied her into the venue, onlookers desperately tried to snap the star on their phones.

The outing came shortly it was announced the E! network had commissioned a spin off reality show for the star called The Life of Kylie.

“These couple years have been such an incredible journey with the support of my fans,” the reality star said.
“This show will allow me to give them a peek inside all of the exciting things I am working on as well as some personal time with friends,” she added in a statement.

The show is set to debut this summer with cameras capturing her at home and work and will run for eight episodes.

The announcement comes less than a year since Kylie said she was sick of fame and wanted to only be a business woman.

And the commissioning of the new series contradicts the fact she said she wanted a life outside of the limelight.

Discussing Keeping Up With The Kardashians with Glamour last year, she said: “The show has to end eventually, and I’m not going to leave until we’re done with it, but by 20 I want to have the whole of my cosmetics line out because that’s my primary focus.”
