Is Cody Being Sent Home?

It’s been a surprisingly quiet week in the Big Brother house.

Part of the reason for that is that ever since Paul became Head of Household, he made his intentions clear and that he would be taking Cody out of the game. 

With him nominating Alex and Josh, the plan was always to take one of those down in order put up Cody. When you think about it, it’s exactly what Cody did to Paul and Christmas last week. 

Karma has a knack for striking in the Big Brother house, and the odds are not stacked in Cody’s favor. Paul won the Veto competition and removed Josh from the block. 

Keeping Alex up there was the better move because there’s a bigger chance Cody could rally the house against Josh because of his unpredictable actions. 

Paul knows there are going to be eight votes to evict Cody this week, so he decided to create some paranoia around the house. 

He pulled Kevin aside yesterday and made a plan with him to cast a vote to evict Ramses. This was because nobody would suspect Kevin of flipping. 

It’s pretty genius, and it would rattle the other houseguests as they prepare for the Head of Household competition.

Paul also spoke to Christmas about bickering with Jessica before the competition in the hopes of throwing her off, but there’s also a chance that Christmas won’t be in the house on Thursday. 

She is set to leave the house at some point today to get surgery on her foot and is expected to return on Thursday in time for the competition. 

However, a picture surfaced on the internet of Christmas taking clothes out of her suitcase. This certainly proves that Christmas thinks her days in the game could be numbered. 

For all we know, she could have been told she will have to leave at some point. I’m sure we’ll get more clarity on it tomorrow, but something tells us she could be making an early exit from the game. 

Tonight’s episode of Big Brother will likely include updates on her condition.
