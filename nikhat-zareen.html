Career

Nikhat participated in the 2011 Women’s Junior and Youth World Boxing Championship after earning the Golden Best Boxer title in 2010. She boxed in the Flyweight weight class. She won a gold medal there against Turkish fighter Ulku Demir. Nikhat Zareen won a Senior title in 2016, then in 2019, she won an International tournament, bringing home a silver medal from the Thailand Open International Boxing Tournament. She is now the Women’s World Champion after capturing a gold medal in 2022. In the Fly-weight division, she beat Thailand’s Jitping Jutaamas. The final competition was placed in Istanbul, Turkey. She is now the sixth lady in India to be crowned Women’s Boxing Champion. Before Nikhat, gold medalists in the Women’s World Boxing Championship were Jeeny R.L., Mary Kom, Lekha K.C., and Laishrma Sarita Devi. Nikhat is now concentrating on her ambition of winning an Olympic gold medal in Rio de Janeiro in 2024.
