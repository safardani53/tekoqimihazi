Heavily pregnant Coleen Rooney wishes husband Wayne happy Valentine's Day in sweet Twitter post

COLEEN Rooney has shown just how strong her marriage to footballer Wayne Rooney is, as she took to Twitter to wish him a happy Valentine's Day.

The heavily pregnant WAG posted an adorable picture of Wayne with their three sons, captioned: "Happy Valentine's Day to my Boys ... have a good one everybody".

The picture appears to have been taken on a family holiday, with her three sons dressed smartly in shorts and t-shirts, whilst she has illustrated the snap with cartoon love hearts.

The happy couple - who started dating aged 16 and got married nine years ago - are parents to sons Kai, eight, Klay, four, and two-year-old Kit.

Her due date is fast approaching with her fourth child understood to be due later this month.

She was thrown a surprise baby shower earlier this month in a gym in Liverpool, posing on exercise bikes with her friends and family for pictures.

She took to Instagram, saying: "Surprise Baby shower yesterday... I absolutely loved it!

"Thanks so much girls and thank you @RiseCycling for perfect venue, @neilnoa.”

Coleen was glowing in the shot and had her hair immaculately styled in soft curls.

She looked radiant in an elegant black two piece, with statement red earrings and a baby shower sash.

However the gender of the baby was kept under wraps - with both blue and pink decorations used to celebrate the day.

The expanded family will live in a £20million Cheshire mansion that is currently being built, which will have a luxury underground pool and gym complex, a cinema room, a garage that parks six cars, a football pitch, and stables large enough for 14 horses.

The six-bedroom mansion will also have two man-made fishing lakes and underground tunnels, and the family are set to move in when building work is finished later this year.

Coleen's sweet message to her husband comes just three months after she opened up about their recent relationship troubles, after Wayne was pulled over for drink driving following a night out in September with party girl Laura Simpson.

Their marriage was thought to be over, and Coleen spoke out about Wayne's "silly and selfish" mistakes and admitted that she thought her marriage "might have been at an end."

But the pair worked through their problems and this is the latest sign that their relationship has turned a corner.
