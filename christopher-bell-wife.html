Morgan Kemenah Bell [2023 Update]

Morgan Bell, the wife of NASCAR racer Christopher Bell, ensures everything she can as a supportive partner & a homemaker when her hubby is busy racing on the track.

She met Christopher in the mid-2010s when the American professional stock car racing driver was climbing the ladder to racing career success.

After dating for more than four years, the pair got engaged in 2018; just a year later of his victory in the 2017 NASCAR Camping World Truck Series.

Then, following two years of the proposal, they tied the knot in 2020 & have been inseparable ever since.

A true definition of a support system is Bell’s wife, Morgan, who frequently visits Christopher on tracks- to cheer him up & probably to pray for him!

Most of her Instagram posts are from the NASCAR tracks, where she is seen with her husband.

While many are familiar with Christopher Bell’s driving career- he drives the No. 20 Toyota Camry for Joe Gibbs Racing, a few know that he is into business ventures too.

With his father-in-law (Morgan’s dad), he founded a new open-wheel racing team.

In this article, we have everything you want to know about the American stock car racing driver’s wife, Morgan Bell. Get details on her family, relationship & net worth.

Who Is Christopher Bell’s Wife, Morgan Bell?

Morgan Bell was born on April 27, 1997, to Stacey and Brian Kemenah. As of 2023, she is 26 years old.

She went to Old Fort High School and graduated in 2015. But further about her education is not out.

Currently, Morgan is working as Pink Status Presenter at Younique Independent Presenter, a cosmetics company that sells makeup and skincare products.

She lives in Mooresville, North Carolina. 

Family History Of Racing

Even though many know Morgan now as the wife of NASCAR racer Christopher Bell, her association with the sport came way earlier- thanks to her family.

Her father, Brian Kemenah, is a crew chief who has expertise as a winged sprint car mechanic.

Currently, he is the crew chief of Bell Kemenah Racing, a new open-wheel racing team, which he co-owns with his son-in-law Christopher Bell.

Morgan’s uncle Chad Kemenah is also a sprint car racer with over 100 victories,  including 27 with the All-Star Circuit of Champions and 5 with the World of Outlaws.

Her father won All-Star Circuit of Champions titles with Chad & Tim Shaffer. He even prepared the car that Shaffer drove to win the 2011 Knoxville Nationals.

Her grandfather Harold Kemenah was also a racing car driver, specifically, a Fremont Speedway racer.

Kemenah’s grandpa started racing in 1966, having started his journey as a car painter for the Hall of Famers like Don Eckhardt, Jim Roepke, and Jim Ford.

Unlike her family, Morgan’s husband Christopher Bell, who is a NASCAR driver, comes from a family with no racing history. His parents introduced him to racing & supported him throughout his journey.

When Did They Start Dating?

The couple began dating in June 2014. At that time, Morgan was 17, and Christopher was 20 years old.

Their first public photo together was shared by Morgan on Instagram in May 2014, taken during her visit to watch Christopher compete in the racetrack.

From Proposal To Wedding Bells

In December 2018, Christopher Bell proposed to Morgan Kemenah after being in a relationship for four and a half years.

Christopher shared a picture of them on Twitter on December 21, 2018, with the caption, “4.5 years later, she said yes! I love you @MKemenah.”

Morgan also posted a picture on Instagram, revealing her ring with a heartfelt caption that read,

“My best friend of 4 and a half years asked, so of course, I said YES! I cannot wait to spend the rest of my life with you.”

Christopher and Morgan got married on February 2, 2020, in a private wedding ceremony at Coley Hall at The Liberty in North Carolina.

The NASCAR driver took to Twitter to announce the news and shared a picture of themselves in their wedding attire.

Their wedding took place just a week before Christopher’s first-ever race at Daytona International Speedway.

The two are yet to share children. On her Instagram bio, Morgan describes herself as a momma to a pet dog named Saide.

Net Worth & Earnings

As of 2023, Christopher Bell’s net worth is estimated to be in the range of $1 million- $2 million.

Primarily, all of his earnings came through his contracts as a stock car racing driver and performance bonuses.

In 2019, he was on a one-year contract with the Leavine Family Racing (LFR) that saw him around $250K in earnings.

Later, the 2023 Food City Dirt Race winner signed a deal with Joe Gibbs Racing (JGR) in 2020 & races for them. It sees him an annual paycheck of $850K.

Other racing car divers signed with the JGR are Martin Truex Jr., Denny Hamlin, & Ty Gibbs.

In January 2023, Bell launched a new open-wheel racing team named Bell Kemenah Racing, which his father-in-law, Brian Kemenah, will lead.

On the other hand, Morgan’s net worth is estimated to be between $50,000 and $100,000, with the majority of her income coming from her work as a Pink Status Presenter.
