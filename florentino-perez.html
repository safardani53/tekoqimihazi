Who is Florentino Perez:

Florentino Perez is a famous Sports Executive. He was born on March 8, 1947 and his birthplace is Spain. Florentino is also well known as, Spanish businessman and former politician best known for being the President of the Real Madrid Football Club. He previously served as the President of the construction company Actividades de Construccion y Servicios, S.A..

Florentino is originated from Spain. His “galacticos” policy has brought some of the biggest names in soccer, including David Beckham, to Real Madrid.
