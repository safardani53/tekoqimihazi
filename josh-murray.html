Josh Murray News, Photos &amp; More Hollywood Life

Bio:
Josh Murray (born Joshua Allan Murray in Syracuse, New York on August 12, 1984) was a contestant on season 10 of  The Bachelorette, competing for Andi Dorfman‘s heart. In 2002, he was a 2nd-round draft pick in the Major League Baseball draft. However, he decided to focus on love and compete on The Bachelorette. In 2016 he competed on season 3 of Bachelor In Paradise and got engaged again. Josh continued his reality television career by appearing on MTV’s The Challenge: Champs vs. Stars in 2017.

Best Known For:
Josh is best known for getting engaged to Andi on The Bachelorette.

Personal Life:
Josh comes from a talented family — his brother is Aaron Murray, a quarterback for the Kansas City Chiefs. He also has a younger sister and an American Pitbull named Sabel. He got engaged to Andi on the season finale of The Bachelorette in 2014, but they split less than a year later and did not end on good terms. He then got engaged to Amanda Stanton on Bachelor in Paradise in summer 2016, but they broke up by the end of the year.
