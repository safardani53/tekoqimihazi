Who is Diego Verdaguer:

Diego Verdaguer is a famous World Music Singer. He was born on April 26, 1951 and his birthplace is Argentina. Diego is also well known as, Mexican singer-songwriter whose best-known songs include “La Ladrona,” “Yo Solamente Yo” and “Corazón de Papel.” He is also known for playing the trumpet as well as the bandoneón.

Diego is originated from Argentina. His song “Voy a Conquistarte” was written by singer Joan Sebastian.
