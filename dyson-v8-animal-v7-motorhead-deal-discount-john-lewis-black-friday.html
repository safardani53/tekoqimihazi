Dyson V8 Animal is on sale at John Lewis ahead of Black Friday, and you can save 100

THE Black Friday sale is the perfect opportunity to upgrade your appliances for a discount price - and there's currently a great deal available at John Lewis.

The retailer is selling the cordless Dyson V8 Animal Complete Cordless Vaccum Cleaner for £299.99.

That's a £100 less than its original price tag of £399.99.

The appliance is perfect for those with furry felines or messy pooches.

Powered by the Dyson Digital V8 motor, the vacuum picks up pet hair and ground in-dirt across both carpets and hard floors with ease.

It's also great for anyone suffering from allergies as it captures pet allergens inside the machine and expels cleaner air.

Although it's far from being cheap, John Lewis' offer is the best one we could find so far.

It also comes with a two year guarantee and free standard delivery with products delivered within five days.
