Jamie Lee Curtis Through the Years: Photos Hollywood Life

Jamie Lee Curtis is 65! Celebrate her birthday and career with photos from through the years.

Jamie Lee Curtis is one of Hollywood’s most beloved actresses. She rose to fame playing Laurie Strode in the 1978 horror classic Halloween, which made her a household name. She returned for six sequels, spanning from 1981 to 2022. Other memorable roles include Trading Places, True Lies, Christmas With the Kranks, and more.

Introducing herself to a new generation, she played Tess Coleman in the 2003 version of Freaky Friday with Lindsay Lohan. Playing with her horror roots, she appeared in Ryan Murphy’s Scream Queens in 2015. Recent favorites include Knives Out (2019) and Everything Everywhere All At Once (2022.)
