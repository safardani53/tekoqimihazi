Towie's Megan McKenna returns to her roots as a sexy barmaid in debut music video

MEGAN McKenna returned to her roots as a barmaid as she shot the sexy new music video for her hit single High Heeled Shoes.

In a saloon-style bar, the pretty brunette stuns in a busy red frilled dress teamed with brown boots as she serves up drinks to punters.

The Towie star, 24, previously worked as a barmaid at Essex hangout Nu Bar where clientele included premiership footballers.

And she acted like a pro on the set of her new vid where she flirted with a handsome cowboy and posed next to a group of denim-clad hunks.

She was also spotted sitting on some steps wearing a cowboy hat and giant hooped earrings.

Later, the star swapped frocks to step out in a pretty white frilled number teamed with a pair of cowboy boots.

But the video set was a far cry from Nashville  - being shot in the more quaintly English setting of Kent with mocked up Wild West buildings.

Earlier this week the song - Megan's debut single - shot to number one on the iTunes chart.

The Towie star's track has now outsold Taylor Swift, Pink and Dua Lipa following its release on Tuesday.

Adding to the Essex favourite's joy was another album track, Far Cry From Love, taking the number five spot on the chart, while the two tracks topped the separate country chart as well.

An overwhelmed Megan, 24, took to social media on Wednesday morning to thank fans for their support and admit it was a "dream come true" to see her own songs being downloaded and listened to.

"[This] is literally my dream come true!" she gushed.

"I can't thank you all enough for downloading my music and supporting me throughout this journey!"

She added on Twitter: "OMGGGGGG IM NUMBER 1 on itunes!!! Thankyou soooooooo much! I love you all!"

Also singing her praises was boyfriend Pete Wicks, who gushed on Twitter: "She's only gone and done it! #Number1."

Her songs were released following the final episode of her ITVBe reality show, There's Something About Megan, which documented her bid to break the lucrative country music market in the US.

She travelled to Nashville for an extended period over the summer to work with country music singers and song-writers and perform a showcase for record label bosses.

In the last episode, which aired on Tuesday evening, the star performed four songs at her showcase in front of an audience of very important people connected to the music industry.

She admitted being nervous before taking to the stage at the famous Station Inn in a bid to secure a record deal - but it paid off as she wowed the crowds with her voice.

"I feel like my final performance went really well," said Megan.

"Walking off the stage and seeing my mum and everyone there, them being so proud and happy about it, it's just the best feeling ever."

Megan did what was needed to impress the record label and was told after that High Heeled Shoes would be released as a single.

Talent agent Nick told her: "The amazing thing was, when you started to sing, you pulled the crowd right in and that's what a great performer would do.

"Tonight you showed me you won over the audience which is very hard to do in this town and in America and the world.

"The great thing about you is you have to be first or you have to be different.

"There's no question you're different but you could also be a first from your area of the world to be successful as a country musician."

Megan exclusively told The Sun Online earlier this week: "What people don't know is that I've been making music myself for years as a hobby.

"I've been working with a lot of different producers and recording things.

"I've never really had a chance to talk about it before. Because I'm a reality star people don't think I'm so passionate about it and don't take me seriously but this is all I've ever wanted to do.

“It's not just about a TV show for me. It's a dream of mine.

"Yes it is another reality show but it is with the hope of launching a music career. I did Ex On The Beach and fell into reality TV but music is what I've always loved doing.”
