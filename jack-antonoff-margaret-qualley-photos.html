Jack Antonoff and Margaret Qualley have been going strong since 2021! Look back at some of the couple’s cutest photos together here.

After high profile relationships with other celebrities, Jack Antonoff and Margaret Qualley have found each other. The lovebirds started dating in the summer of 2021, and were first publicly linked when photos showed them kissing in Brooklyn that August. After various sightings throughout the next several months, they made their public debut in March 2022. From there, they went on to continue attending public events together, including the Grammy Awards in 2022 and 2023.

In the spring of 2022, Jack and Margaret got engaged. The news was confirmed after she was photographed wearing an engagement ring while attending the 2022 Cannes Film Festival in May. The couple then got married in August 2023. For the most part, Jack and Margaret do not publicly discuss their relationship, but the photos of them together have always said it all!
