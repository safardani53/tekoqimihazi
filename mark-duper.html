Who is Mark Duper:

Mark Duper is a famous Football Player. He was born on January 25, 1959 and his birthplace is Louisiana. Mark is also well known as, Wide receiver nicknamed “Super Duper” who made three Pro Bowls during his eleven-year career with the Miami Dolphins. In 2003, he was inducted into the Miami Dolphins Honor Roll.

Mark is originated from United States. He caught passes from Hall of Fame quarterback Dan Marino in Miami.
