RHOSLC's Meredith Marks and Mary Cosby's dramatic reunion gowns slammed as 'a mess' in new photos re

Real Housewives Of Salt Lake City fans got their first-look at the dramatic gowns worn on the upcoming reunion and have slammed the ensembles as “a mess” online. 

On Friday, Bravo released the dresses worn by Jen Shah, Meredith Marks, Mary Cosby, Lisa Barlow, Heather Gay and Whitney Rose for their highly-anticipated season one special. 

Meredith shocked fans with her look, wearing a unique low-plunging skintight dress that pushes her cleavage together. 

The chest is centered between two black lapels with four buttons underneath, with the gold piece going down to her knee on one side and down to her ankle on the other. 

Controversial Housewife Mary dazzled in a long-sleeved gold dress that’s covered in sequins. 

She added her specific touch to the outfit with a fur scarf wrapped around her neck over the gown with matching wrist cuffs also sewn on. 

Fan-favorite, Heather, covered up in a long-sleeve blush dress that was decked out with rose gold and silver embellishments. 

Meanwhile, Whitney put her chest on full display in a ultra-low plunging merlot colored dress. 

The gown had a full satin skirt and had sequins at the top and going done her arms. 

Lisa rocked her signature middle part with her wavy brown hair and wore a sophisticated orange silk dress. 

The one-shouldered number tied at her waist and was complete with a slit for a sexy flare. 

The “Queen” of Park City, Jen, unsurprisingly sparkled with a sequin-covered dress. 

Her outfit had feathers on the bottom and revealed her entire left leg with a high sexy split. 

Jen completed the look with a top bun, diamond necklace and smokey eye from her glam team.

Despite putting in a lot of effort to the first-ever RHOSLC reunion, viewers felt the Utah women  - specifically Mary and Meredith - missed the mark.

“Meredith failed so hard. I’m disappointed,” one disapproving critic tweeted. 

Another wrote: “Lisa, Whitney & Heather look fabulous!  Mary is her typical over-done mess & Jen & Merideth seem to be in their typical weekly uniform- but ala-reunion dress.”

“Heather, Whitney, and Lisa take top honors. What in the world is Meredith wearing. Jen needs to retire the puffy shoulders. Mary. Mary. No,” a third fired off. 

Many were not impressed with any of the outfits: “Honestly I'm disappointed in all of these looks.”

Earlier this week, Bravo boss Andy Cohen announced that he was filming the much-buzzed about reunion with the ladies of Salt Lake City in person with Covid protocols put in place. 

Fans have lots of questions from this explosive season filled with drama centered around outspoken Jen.

In last week’s episode, Jen threw a glass and stormed out of a birthday party for her husband amid her feud with Mary.
