Jennifer Garners Fashion Through the Years: Pics

America’s sweetheart! Ever since Jennifer Garner burst onto the small screen more than two decades ago, she’s been slaying every fashion look she serves.

The 13 Going on 30 star first stole our hearts in 2001 when the hit show Alias premiered. Garner played Sydney Bristow, an undercover spy for the CIA, in the thriller series and she went on to star in several movies and TV shows including Juno, The Last Thing He Told Me, Yes Day, Miracles from Heaven and more.

Just like her stellar acting career, Garner — who shares three kids with ex-husband Ben Affleck — has commanded attention on the red carpet with her keen eye for fashion.

When it comes to premieres and awards show dressing, Garner always knocks it out of the park with jewel-tone gowns by high-fashion designers like Versace and Oscar de la Renta, chic updos and jaw-dropping jewelry. One of her most talked about looks came at the Baby2Baby gala in November 2021. Garner rocked a Prada number featuring a strapless neckline, A-line skirt and knee-high slit. The Ghosts of Girlfriends Past star paired the black gown with a metallic green clutch, emerald necklace and matching diamond earrings.

For glam that night, the Valentine’s Day actress donned a matte face, smokey eyeshadow, glossy lips, rosy cheeks and subtle contour. Her brunette locks were curled in beachy waves and to make the look even more glamorous, the Dallas Buyers Club star swept her bangs to one side of her forehead.

More recently, the Love, Simon actress looked radiant in a Gabriela Hearst ensemble at The Last Thing He Told Me premiere in April 2023. The strapless maxi dress featured a bodycon fit with gold embellishments that made Garner pop even more on the red carpet. She teamed the getup with her shoulder length hair pushed back, dainty earrings and minimal makeup.

Garner doesn’t only have killer red carpet looks — she looks just as classy while out and about. In May 2023, the Tribes of Palos Verdes actress was spotted picking up her son, Samuel, from school in a classy zip-up jacket, camo pants and bright red sneakers. She added just the right amount of sparkle with delicate gold necklaces and completed the outfit with brown shades.
