Doritos Blazin' Buffalo &amp; Ranch: Where to get the limited-edition chips

DORITOS is back with its popular Blazin' Buffalo & Ranch flavored chips.

The flavor's revival, however, is in support of black changemakers.

Where can you get the limited-edition chips?

Doritos' limited-edition Blazin' Buffalo & Ranch chips are available to snag at certain, limited locations.

To find the chips, you can use Doritos' 'Where To Buy' feature on their website.

However, to get your hands on the SOLID BLACK bags, you must be quick.

There are only 2,000 of the limited-edition SOLID BLACK bags online, according to Delish.

Read More on The US Sun

Why is the packaging different?

Doritos Blazin' Buffalo & Ranch chips are taking a new look as the company continues its celebration and support of Black Changemakers for the second year in a row.

According to the bag's artists, Mz. Icar, an anonymous art collective of primarily Black women, the artwork represents:

"a future leaning collective ethos of blackness. We are channeling call and response. We are channeling 'throw it back'. We are channeling raising the vibration through a collective consciousness," they wrote on Instagram.

"This piece is a love letter saying, ' I see you. We see you. We see us.' The green on the bottom represents foundational growth. The fluorescent colors on the top represent possibility and vibrancy the faces blend into one another because our existence requires us to do so in a radically, empathetic and collective manner."

Most read in Money

The brand's SOLID BLACK program will be investing $5million into this year's Black Changemakers campaign.

Doritos will invest $2,000 in each of this year's Changemakers and $125,000 to a nonprofit of their choice.

Who are Doritos' Changemakers?

Doritos' Changemakers include:

  • Miss Diddy: entertainment marketer
  • Justin Rogers, aka DJ Mr Rogers: DJ, producer, and radio mixer
  • Eric Hart Jr: photographer
  • Katie Robinson, also known as 'PikaChulita:' content creator

“When I first heard about the innovative ways these Black Changemakers support their local communities, I couldn’t wait to get involved,” said actress and comedian Ego Nwodim, a partner in the campaign.

“I’m excited to help Doritos celebrate the second year of SOLID BLACK and give these powerful community members the platform they deserve to drive change.”
