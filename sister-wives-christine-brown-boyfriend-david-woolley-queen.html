Christine Brown’s boyfriend David Woolley has called her his “queen” in a sweet Valentine’s Day video.

The Sister Wives star unveiled her new man following her split from Kody in November 2021.

After Christine, 50, decided to finally reveal the identity of her boyfriend on Valentine’s Day, she has now shared her first ever video of David.

In the clip posted to her Instagram Stories on Tuesday, the reality star showed off her new man as they were driving in his car.

“Car confessions!” she began, smiling in the passenger seat.

“I have someone to introduce you to,” said the mom-of-six as she spun the camera around.

Christine finished the video by wishing her followers:“Happy Valentine’s Day!”

‘LOVE OF MY LIFE’

The TLC personality decided February 14 was the right date to introduce fans to her new man after remaining single since her split from Kody.

She posted a series of photos of her and David together, including a loved-up selfie and a shot of them holding her daughter Mykelti’s twins Archer and Ace at a family party.

“Finally found the love of my life, David,” began Christine in the caption.

“The first time he held me close, it felt like my soul took its first breath. He’s wonderful and kind, incredible with my children and an adorable grandpa.

“I never dreamed I could find a love like this," she concluded, adding the hashtag #soulmate.
