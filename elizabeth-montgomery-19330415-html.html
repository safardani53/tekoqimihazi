Elizabeth Montgomery (TV Actress) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Elizabeth Victoria Montgomery was an American actress whose career spanned five decades in film, stage, and television. She is best remembered for her leading role as the witch Samantha Stephens on the television series Bewitched. The daughter of actor, director and producer Robert Montgomery, she began her career in the 1950s with a role on her father's television series Robert Montgomery Presents, and won a Theater World Award for her 1956 Broadway debut in the production Late Love. In the 1960s, she became known for her role as Samantha Stephens on the ABC sitcom Bewitched. Her work on the series earned her five Primetime Emmy Award nominations and four Golden Globe Award nominations. After Bewitched ended its run in 1972, Montgomery continued her career with roles in numerous television films, including A Case of Rape, as Ellen Harrod, and The Legend of Lizzie Borden in the title role. Both roles earned her additional Emmy Award nominations.
