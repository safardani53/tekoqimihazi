UFC fighters Moreno and Pantoja BOTH rushed to hospital after brutal and bloody battle at UFC 290 in

UFC pair Brandon Moreno and Alexandre Pantoja were rushed to hospital after a brutal five-round war in Las Vegas.

Brazilian Pantoja, 33, claimed the UFC Flyweight Championship with a split-decision victory over Mexican Moreno, 29, on Saturday night.

The fight saw 328 strikes landed and 588 thrown as the two men put on a blockbuster show in front of a pro-Moreno crowd at UFC 290.

They were covered in blood when the final bell rang - with both fighters feeling they had done enough to clinch victory.

But it was Pantoja who had his arm raised as two of the three judges scored the contest 48-47 in his favour, with the other seeing it 49-46 for Moreno.

The result led to boos around the T-Mobile Arena as Moreno suffered a THIRD defeat to Pantoja in his MMA career - to go with his 2016 exhibition defeat and 2018 professional loss.
