Is Love Actually a Christmas film?

Love Actually was released in 2003 and is a British-themed Christmas comedy film.

The film, directed by Richard Curtis, follows the lives of eight very different couples in dealing with their love lives.

They all seem to be very loosely interrelated with one another and it is set during a chaotic month before Christmas in London.

The film begins with David (played by Hugh Grant), delivering a voice-over regarding his thoughts on airports and the unbridled joy on the faces of those welcoming their loved ones back from holiday.
