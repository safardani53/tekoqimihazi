Rami Malek, Jamie Dornan and John Cena awarded 'naked Oscars' for best nude scenes of the year

HOLLYWOOD stars Jamie Dornan, Charlie Hunnam and Rami Malek have all been honoured for their sizzling nude scenes in this year's "naked Oscars".

Jamie Dornan, 36, who plays Christian Grey in the Fifty Shades Freed, won Best Butt while former WWE star John Cena took home the Best Buff Butt accolade for baring all in Blockers.

The Best Nude Debut award went to Chris Pine, 38, for getting his kit off in Outlaw King while Brit actor Simon Quarterman, 41, won Best Full Frontal for stripping off in Westworld.

Oscar nominee Rami Malek, 37, who is frontrunner to take home the Best Actor at this year's Oscars got a nod for his steamy shower scene in prison escape movie Papillon alongside Brit Sons of Anarchy star Charlie Hunnam, 38.

Game of Thrones star Peter Dinklage won the "Best Censored" gong for his hilarious naked scene in My Dinner With Herve.

Mr Man was launched in 2004 by the firm behind Mr Skin, a website dedicated to female nude scenes - which was made famous in the movie Knocked Up.

“Hollywood’s A list might be busy getting ready for this year’s Oscars but the award season’s most revealing night is already here  - Mr Man’s annual Manatomy Awards - otherwise known as the naked Oscars," a representative for the site told Sun Online.
