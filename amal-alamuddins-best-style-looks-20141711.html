Amal Clooney's Best Style Moments

Amal Clooney is a woman of many titles; a barrister, a wife, a mother of two children (twins Ella and Alexander, 6) — as well as a style star.

Ever since Amal and husband George Clooney announced their engagement in April 2014, fans have been captivated by the human rights advocate’s impeccable style, both on and off the red carpet.

Although she has been in the public eye for nearly a decade, the core of Amal’s style has remained the same; chic, tailored, and understated are the three words that spring to mind.

From the crisp monochromatic pencil dresses and power suits she wears during work hours, to the elegant floor-length gowns she slips into to join husband George on the red carpet, Amal always seems to know how to show up to events looking effortlessly sophisticated and put together.
