Was Donald Trump in Home Alone 2?

HOME Alone is one of the most iconic Christmas movies of all time.

But fans of the holiday film often wonder if Donald Trump really did appear in the movie's sequel, Lost In New York.

Was Donald Trump in Home Alone 2?

Home Alone 2: Lost in New York is a must-watch Christmas movie.

And Donald Trump did make the final cut of the hit film, which featured a scene in the Plaza Hotel which he owned.

New York’s iconic Plaza Hotel is the place where in the film Kevin McCallister, played by Macaulay Culkin, stays and meets Trump.

The young star runs up a HUGE service bill in the flick.

Home Alone 2 came out in 1992 and made the Plaza even more famous thanks to the iconic scene where Kevin meets Trump in the lobby.

The lad bowls on up to Trump, and asks: "Excuse me, where's the lobby?"

He's told by a steely-eyed Trump: "Down the hall and to the left."

November 2020 marked the 30th anniversary of the classic comedy Home Alone.

Trump celebrated his cameo during a call with troops on Christmas Eve, 2019.

"Well I'm in Home Alone 2," Trump said.

"A lot of people mention it every year, especially around Christmas. They say - especially young kids - they say, 'I just saw you on the movie.' They don't see me on television as they do in the movie.

“But it's been a good movie and I was a little younger, to put it mildly. And it was an honor to do it."

The president continued: “And it turned out to be a very big hit, obviously. It's a big Christmas hit - one of the biggest.

“So it's an honor to be involved in something like that, you always like to see success."

However, when Canadian broadcaster CBC aired the film in 2019, Trump’s star turn was nowhere to be found.

Why was Trump in Home Alone 2?

Home Alone director Chris Columbus says that Donald Trump “bullied” his way into his cameo role in the film’s 1992 sequel, reports Insider.

Trump demands to be written into major productions as a special payment for when films or TV shows are shot on his properties.

Columbus told Insider the production dealt with Trump because, at the time of filming Home Alone 2, he owned the Plaza Hotel.

He recalled: "We wanted to shoot in the lobby - we couldn't rebuild The Plaza on a soundstage."

Canny Trump, however, wanted more than a fee to use the hotel as a setting.

"We paid the fee, but he also said, 'The only way you can use the Plaza is if I'm in the movie'.

"So we agreed to put him in the movie," Columbus added.

And, the only reason he made the cut was that during a screening "people cheered when Trump showed up on-screen", he said.

So the director told his editor to "leave him in... but he did bully his way into the movie".

Matt Damon said there have been many instances Trump was filmed in a scene with stars before it would be later edited out by directors.

What other films has Trump been in?

Matt Damon told The Hollywood Reporter: "The deal was that if you wanted to shoot in one of his buildings, you had to write him in a part.

“Martin Brest had to write something in Scent of a Woman – and the whole crew was in on it.

“You have to waste an hour of your day with a bulls**t shot.

“Donald Trump walks in and Al Pacino's like, 'Hello, Mr Trump!' – you had to call him by name – and then he exits."

Trump's scene was cut from Scent of a Woman, according to star Chris O'Donnell.

The New York business magnate has appeared in TV shows, movies, and even professional wrestling, writes Cinema Blend.
