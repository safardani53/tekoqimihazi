Woman shares recipe for the best slush puppie cocktails youll taste with WKD, Smirnoff AND

THE weekend is nearly upon us and could be the perfect time to whip up a delicious cocktail at home. 

A woman has shared her recipe for the ‘nicest slush puppies you’ll ever taste’ which can be made with Smirnoff, WKD and Hooch. 

She shared a photo of her colourful ice drinks to the Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, and they looked mouth-watering. 

The bottom section of the cocktail is a bright pink colour from pink Hooch, the middle bit is ice-cold Smirnoff and the top section is blue WKD. 

She explained her easy method, and said: “Mix separately in blender with ice. 

“Wkd first then Smirnoff then pink hooch.”

She added that although the cocktail went down a storm, it did give her brain freeze. 

Unsurprisingly, her boozy creations have racked up thousands of likes online, from people keen to have a sip of nostalgia. 

One person raved: “we need to make these.”Another added: “am going to try this tonight.”

Please drink responsibly and be Drinkaware.
