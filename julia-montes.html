Who is Julia Montes:

Julia Montes is a famous Movie Actress. She was born on March 19, 1995 and her birthplace is Pandacan, Philippines. Julia is also well known as, Actress and model who became recognized for her role as Clara in the TV series Mara Clara. She also starred in the show Muling Buksan Ang Puso and played roles in the films A Moment in Time and Halik sa Hangin.

Julia is originated from Philippines. She co-starred with Kathryn Bernardo on the Philippine TV drama series Mara Clara from 2010 to 2011.
