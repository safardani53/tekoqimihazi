Shake Chatterjee Apologizes to Deepti Vempati

Shake Chatterjee has finally issued an apology to Deepti Vempati.

The Love Is Blind star made a video in which he expressed remorse for his behavior on Love Is Blind.

"I’m sorry, @lifewithdeeps . I’m ready to take accountability for my actions and be better," he captioned the clip.

“Deepti, I am truly sorry for some of the things I said.”

Shake seems sincere, but it’s hard to trust the guy … especially since it looks like he made the video in a busy restaurant during his lunch break.

Not surprisingly, commenters were skeptical.

"It’s only because everyone is coming for you…." one person wrote.

They might be onto something.
