Pink: Topless, Pregnant on Instagram!

Question of the day: is Pink is pregnant with her second baby?

Why yes – yes she absolutely is, and isn’t her latest, very naked pregnancy pic just adorable?

(That second question was a redundant one, of course.)

Pink shared the above photo on her Instagram account on Monday night with the caption "?the snuggle is real ? :@deborahandersoncreative hair: @pamwiggy hands: willow sage"

The image features the singer and her daughter, Willow Sage, whose hands you can see wrapped around her mama’s tummy. 

Last month, Pink announced that she was carrying her second child via an Instagram pic which featured her dressed in a flowing white gown, alongside her daughter.

The burgeoning bump pic was simply captioned "Surprise!" 

We were surprised, considering her on-and-off relationship with Carey Hart, to whom she’s married. 

This is hardly the first time Pink has stripped down for the cameras, though. 

Back in 2015, Pink got naked for PETA in an ad shaming those who wear fur, and you know how intense those PETA ads can get. 

At the time of the shoot, the Raise Your Glass singer said, "I’ve always felt that animals are the purest spirits in the world."

"They don’t fake or hide their feelings, and they are the most loyal creatures on Earth," she said.

The ad itself read: "Be comfortable in your own skin, and let animals keep theirs."

Typical PETA fare for the typical PETA audience, yes? 

"I would like to say I’ve always been fur-free," the singer conceded, "but unfortunately, I went through a selfish phase and wore fur on a couple of occasions." 

She got better, though, y’all.

"But I wised up and now boycott fur completely," she said, adamantly.

Continuing in that vein, she said, "I wish everyone was forced to learn the horrors that these animals go through for fashion trends." 

"I hope fur-wearers get bitten in the ass by the same kind of animal they wear on their back."

Whatever you say, Pink — just keep popping out adorable babies and the nude thing? 

Please keep doing that, too. 

Always the naked thing, thanks.
