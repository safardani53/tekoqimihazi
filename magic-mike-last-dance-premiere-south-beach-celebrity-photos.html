Magic Mikes Last Dance Premiere In South Beach Celebrity Photos Hollywood Life

The highly anticipated movie, Magic Mike’s Last Dance, premiered in Florida on Jan. 25, 2023, and the stars showed up in complete style! In attendance at the premiere included Salma Hayek, Channing Tatum, and more iconic stars. This chapter will be the final film in the Magic Mike franchise after the first movie premiered in 2012.

Three years after the first film was released, Channing starred in the sequel, Magic Mike XXL, alongside Joe Manganiello and Matt Bomer. In the third installment, not only does the main character return but so does Matthew McConaughey.

On Oct. 21, 2022, Channing took to Instagram to share a first glimpse at the film, which notably included a snapshot of Salma carefully caressing his abs. “All good things begin in Miami. #MagicMikesLastDance in theaters Valentine’s Day weekend. And #MagicMikeLive’s @mmltour is open now!”, he captioned the post. Later, on Nov. 15, the official trailer for the project was released.
