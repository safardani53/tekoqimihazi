Christian Vande Velde Wife Leah Is A Proud Mom Of Two

Christian Vande Velde and his wife Leah have been married for over 23 years.

They started dating during high school and eventually tied the knot in 2002.

A few years after their marriage, they welcomed their first child, followed by their second a year later.

Leah is a very supportive wife and a proud mother of her kids.

Christian Vande Velde is a retired American professional road racing cyclist who was born on May 22, 1976.

He professionally rode from 198 to 2013 and competed for the US Postal Service, Liberty Seguros, Team SCS, and Garmin-Sharp squads.

He booked several wins throughout his career, including Tour de France (2011) and Giro d’Italia (2008 and 2012) in the Grand Tours.

Moreover, he was involved in a doping controversy that cost him his race results between June 4, 2004, to April 30, 2006, and was suspended for six months.

Nonetheless, his professional career was a big success, and following his retirement, he started working as a cycling analyst for NBC Sports in 2014.

Christian Vande Velde Wife Leah
