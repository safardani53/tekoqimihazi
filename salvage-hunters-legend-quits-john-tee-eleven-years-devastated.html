Salvage Hunters legend quits show in shock move after 11 years onscreen - and fans are devastated

A SALVAGE Hunters legend and OG member of the show has shocked viewers by announcing he is quitting after 11 years onscreen. 

The long-running series follows decorative salvage dealer Drew Pritchard as he travels throughout the UK in search of antiques to re-sell.

Quest, the network that shows Salvage Hunters shared the update with followers on social media.

They wrote: "T has decided to leave #SalvageHunters after 11 years on the road.

"We'd like to thank T for bringing his dry wit and gentle charm to all he has done for the series and wish him all the best in his future endeavours."

John 'T' Tee has been by Drew's side since the beginning driving him from location to location and helping him load the truck.

In the short video, bearded T told viewers that after 11 years he has decided it's time to move on.

He went on: "I've been lucky enough over the years to work with some amazing crew, visited some stunning locations and filmed with some lovely people.

"So thank you all for watching, au revoir. But remember, we'll always have Patina Turner."

Fans of the show were devastated to hear the news and rushed to wish T all the best for the future.
