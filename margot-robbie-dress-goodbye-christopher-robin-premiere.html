Margot Robbie struggles with dress as goes for wet look hair and bright orange eyeshadow at Goodbye

MARGOT Robbie struggled with her dress on the red carpet and shocked her young co-star with her wet-look hair.

The Australian actress continues to take over Hollywood with a leading role in biopic, Goodbye Christopher Robin, the story behind Winnie-the-Pooh author A.A. Milne.

Attending tonight's premiere in London's Leicester Square, Margot brought a touch of glitz and glamour to proceedings.

Donning a full-length floral gown that showed off her incredible figure, Margot took the red carpet by storm but was frequently helped out by assistants as she risked falling out of the frock.

With her hair slicked back, Margot opted for a bizarre orange shade of eyeshadow.

The 27-year-old seemed to shock Will Tilston, her young co-star in Goodbye Christopher Robin.

The young actor was seen looking surprised at Margot's appearance.

Other guests at the star-studded premiere included Strictly Come Dancing hopeful Brian Conley and Made in Chelsea socialite Kimberley Garner.

Taking time out of his intense Strictly Come Dancing training, Brian looked every inch the suave gentleman.

Former reality TV star Kimberley Garner, 27, stunned in eye-catching PVC trousers.

Margot has recently been hanging out with Essex hunk Mark Wright as he continues to launch his US career.

The pair recreated Margot's cheeky Instagram post that confirmed she'd married Tom Ackerley.

Mark captioned the upload: "When you join in with @margotrobbie to recreate THAT iconic pic."

Margot married Tom in December 2016 after they met on the set of Suite Francaise.

Margot, 27, started her career in 2008 with a role in iconic Australian soap, Neighbours.

After a three-year stint on the popular show, Margot made the jump to the Hollywood A-list in 2011 after starring opposite Leonardo DiCaprio in The Wolf of Wall Street.

She's since gone on to land roles in box office hits including Suicide Squad, Focus and The Legend of Tarzan.
