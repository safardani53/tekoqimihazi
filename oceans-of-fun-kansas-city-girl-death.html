Oceans of Fun 'drowning' - Young girl dies in Kansas City after being pulled from water at amusement

A GIRL has died a week after being pulled from a pool at a popular water park, according to the park’s owner on Tuesday.

Law enforcement said that the young girl was pulled from the Coconut Cove pool at the Oceans of Fun water park on July 5.

According to a statement from Cedar Fair Entertainment Co, which owns the park, the girl looked to be in distress before she was taken out of the water.

The girl was taken to Children’s Mercy Hospital in critical condition after fire department officials stepped in to care for her.

Cedar Fair revealed the girl had died, however, other information, including the girl’s name, was not given out.

Cops said the girl was younger than 10 years old.
