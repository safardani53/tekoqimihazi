Rhian Sugden in hospital as she posts from ward and reveals urgent surgery

RHIAN Sugden has revealed she's in hospital as she took to social media with a snap from her bed in a ward.

The model went make-up free as she posed for a selfie in a hospital gown as she headed for an urgent surgery.

Glamour girl Rhian, 36, struck a pout towards the camera as she opened up about a worrying health scare.

The star took to her Instagram with the selfie, which saw her go completely make-up free, to explain she was due to have surgery on a "suspicious birthmark" on her leg.

Rhian - who is best known for her busty snaps - covered up under the hospital gown as she let her brunette locks flow around her.

But while she kept smiling, the star admitted she was "nervous" as she headed down to surgery.

"Holy Moley," Rhian penned to her fans. "My consultant has flagged up that a birthmark on my leg looks very suspicious.

"He has got me in today to have it cut out. It's quite big so I'm nervous. Need up to 12 stitches."

She went on to encourage her fans: "Always make sure you keep an eye on changing birthmarks and moles.

"If they look dodgy as hell, get them whipped off. It's better to be safe than sorry."
