YOU may be able to grab free beauty samples at Target, but it's only in one place.

The tip comes from Target employee and TikToker Kaitlin Sondae.

Kaitlin shares all types of helpful information for Target shoppers like when to expect sales, new product alerts, and other helpful shopping hacks.

She recently took to TikTok and revealed that shoppers can easily score free beauty samples - but they are only in the beauty aisle.

Some Target locations will put them on displays in that section so that they are easily accessible, while other stores may be upon request only.

However, just note that not every Target offers free samples so her trick may only apply to some stores.

FREE SAMPLE PRODUCTS

In another TikTok, Kaitlin revealed the types of sample products her store typically receives.

She shared that in a recent shipment, the store got Proactive Acne Body Wash, Dickinson's Witch Hazel toner, Vital Proteins packs, and Dove lotion.

Her location also received a mini E.L.F. primer, CeraVe lotion and moisturizer, hair ties, and Palmer's skin oil.

While there are many more products the store offers, Kaitlin said in a TikTok comment that if you cannot find any, politely ask a store employee as each month some stores get new products.

WAYS TO SAVE AT TARGET

Target has an app that shoppers may use to browse and clip digital coupons specific to their store location.

In addition, you may be able to spot discounts by scanning items' barcodes through the Target app.

The app can show you when prices are marked down, even if the label hasn't been changed in-store, so you can score savings you'd otherwise miss by utilizing that tool.

Plus the Target price match guarantee states that the store will match the price if you buy a qualifying item at Target and then find the identical item for less at:

  • Target.com
  • Select online competitors
  • In Target’s or competitor’s local print ad

Price matches may be requested at the time of purchase or within 14 days after purchase. 

The full list of online competitors is available online.
