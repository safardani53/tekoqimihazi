The Bold and the Beautiful fans think Finn is ALIVE after star Tanner Novlan shows off script in new

THE Bold and the Beautiful fans have started speculating that Finn is actually alive after noticing a clue on star Tanner Novlan’s social media.

Viewers have been spiraling ever since Finn was seemingly killed by his mom Sheila earlier this month.

Tanner, 37, continued to get fans’ hopes up that his character might not be dead after he shared a cryptic new post on Instagram.

The Bold and the Beautiful star smoldered for the camera as he held onto a script in his hands.

The actor captioned the photo: “All these lines but no stress. Stress, as we all know, comes at us all day, every day.”

Fans quickly clocked the stack of papers in the snapshot and jumped into the comments to speculate that it’s actually a new Bold script.

SHOCK DEATH

Finn's death storyline began earlier this month when Finn's wife, Steffy, watched as her husband was accidentally shot by his biological mother, Sheila.

Steffy had learned that Sheila caused Stef's step-mom, Brooke, to relapse by switching out her non-alcoholic champagne with the real version and told Finn she was going to confront her.

He became increasingly worried about the mother of his child when she wouldn’t respond to his texts and decided to leave work to rush over the Italian restaurant he knew they'd be at.

Finn got there just as Sheila had raised a gun at Steffy out back by Il Giardino's dumpster, and he jumped in front and was shot instead.

Shocked, Steffy ran over to try to wake her unconscious husband and to try to stop all of the blood, screaming as she realized how much there was.

As she worked to dial 9-1-1 for help, Sheila once again pointed the gun at her and forbade her from finishing the call.

The next episode, though, Sheila still went on to shoot Steffy, leaving both presumably dead.

Steffy was later revealed to be alive, though with amnesia, while Finn's supposedly dead.

DEAD OR ALIVE?

At the time, Finn was said to be dead by various characters, including Deacon, who found him and spoke to EMTs at the scene.

Since then, all of the characters have adopted the Finn-is-dead attitude, though fans aren't quite there yet.

Aside from Tanner's latest post that features a script, his followers are also speculating his character's still alive after spotting other clues, ones on the show.

A theory formed last week after fans noticed some possible evidence in scenes with with Finn's adoptive mom, Li.

A few scenes with Dr. Li Finnegan (Naomi Matsuda) left fans suspicious that more is going on behind the scenes with Finn than viewers are currently aware of.

For one, fans noted that instead of being an emotional, crying mess over the death of her son, she's only extremely angry.
