Meet Nev Schulman's wife Laura Perlongo

NEV Schulman is known for hosting and producing MTV's reality show Catfish.

Now, fans are curious to learn more about Nev's wife, Laura Perlongo.

Who is Nev Schulman's wife, Laura Perlongo?

Laura Perlongo was born on September 6, 1985, in Michigan.

She has degrees in Commercial and Advertising Art and Social Psychology from Boston University.

Laura's LinkedIn bio says she is a "freelance millennial-focused creative based in New York & LA."

The bio continues: "Past clients include Calvin Klein, Derek Lam, Target, Vespa, Stella Artois, NBC, BBC, Chevy, Dewar's, Diamonds, Bloomberg, Barbie, etc."

How did Laura first meet Nev?

Laura and Nev first came into contact in 2015 when she was interested in purchasing a bike he was selling.

According to Us Weekly, she didn't buy the bike but accepted a date offer from Nev.

The Catfish star told the outlet he immediately ended things with Laura because he was "too in love" with her.

He said: “I just got out of a thing, and I had been damaged by that, and I felt that maybe I was all messed up, and I wasn’t ready.

"I screwed it up and then I had to win her back.”

When did Laura and Nev get married?

The couple got engaged in May 2016 upon learning they were pregnant with their daughter Cleo James.

Cleo was born on October 21, 2016, and the couple married on July 22, 2017.

The wedding was in Nev's father's backyard in East Hampton, New York.

Since then, Nev and Laura have had two more children: Beau in 2019 and Cy in 2021.
