Who is Mike D’Antoni:

Mike D’Antoni is a famous Basketball Coach. He was born on May 8, 1951 and his birthplace is Mullens, WV. Mike is also well known as, NBA coach who became known for his high-scoring offenses with the Phoenix Suns. He later went on to coach the New York Knicks, Los Angeles Lakers and Houston Rockets. He was named NBA Coach of the Year in 2005 and 2017. 

Mike is originated from United States. He knew Kobe Bryant from their days in Italy and the two became reunited when D’Antoni became coach of the Lakers.
