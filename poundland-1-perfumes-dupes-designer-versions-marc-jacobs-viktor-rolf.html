Poundland reveals which 1 perfumes are exact dupes of pricey designer versions including Marc Jaco

POUNDLAND has two new perfumes available in store which are exact dupes of pricey designer versions including Marc Jacobs & Viktor & Rolf.

But like with all of the bargain fragrances the store has on offer, they cost just £1.

Lily Petals is one of the two new women's scents and is said you smell just like the popular Marc Jacobs Daisy, which sells for £59 for a 50 ml bottle.

Top notes include violet leaf, blood grapefruit and strawberry and has hints of vanilla and Jasmine too.

Meanwhile, Fleur Bloom has been compared to Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb, but, of course, for a fraction of the price

The pricey version will set you back £56 for a 50ml, and that's have the size of Poundland's cheap £1 buy.

In January, the store dropped a whole new line of scents - all for a quid - and shoppers have been raving about them since.

Others in the range include Black Dusk, Rose, Sparkling Sky, 9th Element, Beau Reve and more, and fans have noticed similarities to their favourite brands. 

Black Dusk has been compared to YSL’s Black Opium, which sells for £54 a bottle on the brand's website.

Meanwhile 9th Element has been hailed as a dead ringer for Chanel No. 5, costing £57 from their official website. 

As Poundland doesn’t sell online, you’ll need to head to your nearest store to bag yourself a bottle.
