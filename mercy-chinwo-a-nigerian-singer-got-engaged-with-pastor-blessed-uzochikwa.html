Mercy Chinwo A Nigerian Singer Got Engaged With Pastor Blessed Uzochikwa

Mercy Chinwo, a Nigerian musician, is recently engaged to Pastor Blessed Uzochikwa of The WaterBrook Church. On June 23, Chinwo announced her engagement with a couple of images of herself with Uzochikwa on her social media site. Chinwo wrote on Instagram that she was “fortunate to be blessed” with Uzochikwa. Uzochikwa, on the other side, praised God in his Instagram post while expressing his love for Chinwo.

Mercy Chinwo’s fiancé’s career, financial worth, and other information

Blessed Uzochikwa is a resident pastor of The Waterbrook Church who rose to prominence after his engagement to Mercy Chinwo. He is a Delta State native from Nigeria’s southern area who presently resides in Lagos. He is also a human rights activist who backed South Africa’s battle against xenophobia in 2020.

He graduated from the University of Port Harcourt with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. He also attended Stanford University and studied Stanford Advanced Project Management. Uzochikwa is a Microsoft-certified IT expert with a certificate in advanced project management. Uzochikwa has worked at Tetra Pak as an IT administrator and MicrowareSolutions/IBM Partners as a Network Engineer. He later joined AstraZeneca as an IS/IT Business Support for South Africa and Sub Sahara Africa. He was promoted to the position of Associate Project Manager. According to internet sources, his net worth is believed to be over $25,000, yet his source of income is unclear.

All About Mercy Chinwo

Mercy Chinwo, who was born on September 5, 1991, started her singing career at the age of eight. She rose to prominence after being crowned the winner of Nigerian Idols. In 2015, she released her debut song, Testimony, followed by Igwe in 2016. The Cross: My Gaze, her first studio album, was released in February 2018 and included guest performances by Chris Morgan, Olaitan Odoko, Shady B, and Fiokee. Chinwo made her acting debut in the comedy flick House of Gold. She is presently represented by EeZee Concept.
