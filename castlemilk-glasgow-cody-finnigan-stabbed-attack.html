Glasgow man fights for life after being stabbed six times in Castlemilk revenge attack by craz

A MAN is fighting for his life after a feared revenge attack by a crazed knifeman.

Cody Finnigan, 29, was stabbed six times in the bloodbath murder bid.

Locals in Castlemilk, Glasgow, claimed the victim was targeted after an alleged incident with a woman and her boyfriend outside shops near his home on Sunday.

He was left for dead in the street at 5.30pm the following day.

A neighbour said: “Finnigan crossed a line and has paid a heavy price. It’s touch and go whether he will make it.”
