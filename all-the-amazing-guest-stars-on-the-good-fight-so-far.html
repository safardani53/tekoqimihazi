All The Amazing Guest Stars On The Good Fight, So Far

Who doesn't enjoy a lovable rogue? In The Good Fight, that's Tully Nelson, a rough-around-the-edges political firebrand and protestor who Diane meets in "Day 429," when he bartends a party hosted at the Reddick, Boseman & Kolstad law offices.

That's just the beginning of their relationship, however. The two go home together and in the following episode, Diane has to bail Tully (Tim Matheson) out of jail after he's arrested while getting up to some protest-related hi-jinks.

He makes another appearance in "Day 457," this time when Diane has to appear at his bail hearing for incitement to riot and resisting arrest.

Diane's dalliances with Tully come back to haunt her, however, when federal prosecutor Patrick Basehart attempts to indict her for conspiracy to assassinate the president based on a recording of Tully where Diane was present.
