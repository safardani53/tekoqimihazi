How many kids does Kevin Costner have?

KEVIN Costner has had a highly successful career in the entertainment industry.

The accomplished actor and musician has multiple kids with three different women.

How many kids does Kevin Costner have?

Kevin Costner, 68, has seven kids, including three daughters and four sons.

He has three adult children — including Annie, Lily, and Joe Costner — whom he shares with his ex-wife Cindy Silva, 66.

39-year-old Annie was born on April 15, 1984, in Los Angeles, California.

As per People, the actress and producer co-founded Sound Off Films in 2014.

Kevin and Cindy welcomed Lily on August 4, 1986, in Los Angeles.

The 36-year-old actress and singer sang a duet of Heaven's Gate with her dad's band, Kevin Costner & Modern West.

The track appears on the album Tales from Yellowstone.

Like his older sisters, Joe was also born in Los Angeles. Kevin and Cindy welcomed him on January 31, 1988.

According to his LinkedIn profile, he works as an audio engineer and a production sound mixer in the entertainment industry.

As People notes, Joe also has acting experience. As a child, he appeared in the movie Tin Cup with Kevin, before starring in The Postman alongside his dad and older sisters.

Kevin shares son Liam Costner with his former partner, Bridget Rooney, with whom he had a short-lived romance.

26-year-old Liam was born in November 1996 and "generally stays out of the spotlight," as per People.

Kevin shares his three youngest children — Cayden Wyatt, Hayes Logan, and Grace Avery Costner — with his wife, 49-year-old Christine Baumgartner, who has since filed for divorce from the Yellowstone star.

Cayden, 15, was born on May 6, 2007, in Los Angeles, while 14-year-old Hayes was born on February 12, 2009, in the same city.

The former couple welcomed Grace, 13, on June 2, 2010, in California.

What is Kevin Costner's net worth?

Kevin's successful career as an actor, musician, film director, and producer has earned him a high net worth.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, his fortune is valued at $250million.

Who are Kevin Costner's exes?

Kevin Costner has been married twice.

He wed his first wife, Cindy, in 1978. The pair divorced in 1994.

The Los Angeles Times reported that Cindy and Kevin were college sweethearts from Cal State Fullerton.

Cindy is an actress featured in Dances with Wolves and LiteWeight, as per her IMDb profile.

Following his split from his first wife, Kevin dated Bridget, though the pair never married.

As per People, her uncle is Dan Rooney, the former owner of the NFL's Pittsburgh Steelers. Today notes that her grandfather is Art Rooney, the football team's "founding owner."

In September 2004, the TV star married his second wife, Christine.

Christine is a handbag designer for the label Cat Bag Couture, as People reported.

As per the publication, the pair met on a golf course where Kevin was rehearsing for his 1996 film, Tin Cup.
