A Former AOA Member Jimin Joined A MALO Entertainment

Jimin, a former AOA member, announced joining a new agency and resuming her artistic pursuits on July 14. ALO MALO Entertainment, the artist’s new agency, published a statement stating that they have inked an exclusive deal with the singer. Jimin also claimed via her agency that she spent time thinking about her history. She stated that she is cautious but intends to take things slowly. She also asked followers to keep an eye out for her future endeavors.
