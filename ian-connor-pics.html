Ian Connor: Pics Hollywood Life

The 27-year-old New York native is a stylist, model, and influencer whose face and name started popping up all over Hollywood when he was adopted by the A$AP Mob (aka ASAP Rocky, ASAP Ferg, ASAP Twelvyy, ASAP Nast and ASAP Ant). 

He started out his modeling career for small, lesser known brands like Praise, and then eventually went onto model for the likes of Mishka, Pyrex, Stussy, Gosha Rubchinskiy, VETEMENTS, Palace, and Supreme. Oh yeah, and how could we forget he’s also become one of Yeezy’s most prominent models. 

He’s walked for both Yeezy Season 1 and Season 2. Presentation, where he garnered much attention for his modeling antics, such as casually strutting the runway while smoking a cigarette. 

As a stylist, he also continues to make a name for himself, working with celebrities and rappers like Kylie Jenner and Wiz Khalifa.
