A Chinese Man Who looks Like Elon Musk- Photos

On the internet, a guy has gone famous for having the exact spitting likeness of Elon Musk. People are Calling him Chinese Elon Musk. The guy is standing next to a black automobile and gazing into the camera in a video released on Douyin, China’s equivalent of TikTok. The resemblance between this anonymous person and the tech millionaire who created Tesla, PayPal, and SpaceX is remarkable. People in the comments section have dubbed him Yi Long Musk, and he’s taken the internet by storm.

‘Wanna go for a ride?’

In a KL video, the ‘Chinese Elon Musk’ asks whether anyone is ready for a ride. The man’s looks, body structure, and speaking style resembled Elon Musk’s, prompting speculation he was his long-lost twin. WeirdKaya is hunting for the original video despite it being on Douyin.

People Assume It’s Deepfake Video

Someone claimed that the video was a deepfake, which is a sophisticated technique that enables users to create a convincing face on someone else’s body. There’s no way to know for sure since we don’t know who the person in the video is, so we can’t ask him to show it’s his face.

Anything Replica Can Be Found In China

Because China is the country where many products are manufactured, Facebook users have commented on how you can really get anything from China! You can even get your own Elon Musk through Taobao, a Chinese online shopping platform.

Short Description About Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk is a businessman and entrepreneur. He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Engineer of SpaceX; an early-stage investor, Tesla, Inc.’s CEO and Product Architect; the founder of The Boring Company; and the co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI. Musk is the world’s wealthiest person, with an estimated net worth of around US$255 billion as of December 2021[2].
