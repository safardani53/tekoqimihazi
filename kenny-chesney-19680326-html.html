Kenny Chesney (Country Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Kenneth Arnold Chesney is an American singer, songwriter, guitarist, musician, dancer and record producer. He has recorded more than 20 albums. He has also produced over 40 top 10 singles on the US Billboard Hot Country and Country Airplay charts, 32 of which reached number one. Many of them also entered the US Billboard Hot 100 Top 40, making him one of the most successful country music artists. He has sold over 30 million albums worldwide. Chesney co-directed and produced a film for ESPN called The Boys of Fall. He has received six Country Music Academy awards, as well as nine Country Music Association awards. He is one of the most popular touring artists in the country music industry, regularly selling the venues where he performs. Its 2007 Flip-Flop summer tour was the highest-grossing country road trip of the year.
