Inside RHOSLC star Whitney Rose's stunning Utah mansion featuring a movie theater, bar and pole danc

REAL Housewives of Salt Lake City star Whitney Rose gave Bravo fans an inside look into her stunning Utah mansion.

The luxurious home features a movie theater, bar and pole dancing room.

The 38-year-old old welcomed RHOSLC fans into her home, which she shares with her husband Justin and their two children Bobbi and Brooks.

Her home features a spacious family room, which is completed with a large white couch and tan chairs.

White fluffy blankets sit on the tan chairs, while the couch is decorated with neutral colored pillows.

A low brown coffee table sits in the middle of the room, while a large television is hung on the wall above a black fireplace.

The room also has light gray chairs placed in front of large windows that offer nice views of the impressive backyard.

Whitney loves the living room because of its "open concept and the natural light."

The Bravo star also shared that the kitchen is her "favorite room in the house."

When designing the kitchen, Whitney made sure the room included a big island and a large stovetop and range with a pot filler.

In addition to the black island and appliances, the kitchen features white and light wooden cabinets.

For the bedroom, Whitney wanted it to have a "sexy, chic, relaxed environment."

A large white bed with a gray headboard is placed in the center of the room, while the walls are covered with black, pink and green floral wallpaper.

Downstairs in the basement, a large leather couch is placed around a black fireplace.

The basement also features an impressive bar stocked with a number of liquor bottles placed on shelves.

The RHOSLC star concluded the tour by showing viewers her stripper pole, which is located in the family's home gym.

Set near a collection of weights, the pole is located at the center of the room.

The TV personality explained she uses to pole to get "creative" and "workout."

Additionally, the house also features a home theater so that the family can spend quality time together watching movies.
