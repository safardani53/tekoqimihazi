‘Being Dad: A Teen Mom Special’ Preview: Jo & Vee’s Relationship Takes A Hit

Uh oh! Trouble in paradise for Jo and Vee?

The couple, who moved in with each other during season five of Teen Mom 2, seem to be at a standstill during this clip from the upcoming Father’s Day special.

While out to dinner with Jo’s son, Isaac, the couple get into a bit of an argument over where their relationship is headed. Apparently, Vee has been asking Jo when they are going to get engaged, but Jo’s honest response is not what she wanted to hear at all.

‘Being Dad’ Will Also Go Behind-The-Scenes

Not only is Jo participating in the Being Dad special, but Corey Simms and Ryan Edwards will also return.

The exciting Father’s Day special will also take a look behind-the-scenes at the lives of Teen Mom 2‘s dads.

“Being Dad will have the same ‘behind-the-scenes’ feel as the Being Maci and other specials and was not shot in front of a studio audience. The interviews were taken at the guys’ homes and feature candid conversations about fatherhood,” reports reality television writer The Ashley.

It will definitely be interesting to see things from the dad’s perspective for once.
