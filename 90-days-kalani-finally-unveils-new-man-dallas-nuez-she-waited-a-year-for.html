90 Days Kalani finally unveils new man Dallas Nuez she waited a year for Wed 1 N

90 Day Fiance spin off The Last Resort star Kalani Faagata has confirmed the dating rumors surrounding her and a man named Dallas Nuez. The mom of two headed to Florida to a couples retreat in order to save her marriage with Asuelu Pulaa but the two weren’t able to make things work. Now, she appears to have moved on with Dallas, per her Instagram Stories.

Dallas Nuez was Kalani’s ‘hall pass’ on the show

During TLC‘s 90 Day The Last Resort, Kalani and Asuelu’s marriage was on the rocks.

The two aired their marital issues around infidelity and attempted to work through their past.

Because of Asuelu’s past cheating, Kalani was granted a “hall pass” on the show.

The man Kalani spends the night with is named Dallas Nuez. She explains on the show she’s “not the type of person that can sleep with someone and just forget about them.”

She adds she never intended to stay in touch with Dallas and wanted to repair her marriage with Asuelu on the 90 Day Fiancé spin-off show.

Kalani confirms dating rumors

After Kalani and Dallas Nuez remained in contact after her hall pass, dating rumors began to swirl around the two.

Towards the end of their time at the resort, Kalani left to spend more time with Dallas. Asuelu explained that he had no idea where his wife had gone.

Kalani said she was “torn” during the show. She wasn’t sure if she could “bring herself to forgive” her husband for his past actions.

Now that 90 Day The Last Resort has finished, Kalani has seemingly confirmed the dating rumors with Dallas via an Instagram Story.

She ‘waited a year’ for him

She shared an image of Dallas in the sea with his back to the camera. Kalani captioned the picture: “I waited a year for this.”

She tags her rumored partner in the post who has an account with zero posts at the time of writing – @dallas.n714. It could also be a private account.

Speaking on the TLC show, Kalani said that Dallas “opened her eyes to a different world,” after spending a lot of time “crying” over Asuelu.
