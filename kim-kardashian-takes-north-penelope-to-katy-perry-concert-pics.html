Kim Kardashian Takes North, Penelope to Katy Perry Concert: Pics

Girls’ night out! Kim Kardashian hit the town with daughter North West, niece Penelope Disick and their pals, who all were captivated by Katy Perry’s Las Vegas residency.

“I surprised the girls with [tickets] to the @katyperry concert! I think I’m more excited [than] anyone,” the Skims mogul wrote via Instagram Story on Saturday, April 15, sharing footage of the squad’s travels via her private jet, which was complete with custom mocktails and cotton candy treats.

Once the plane touched down in Vegas, Kardashian arranged for a party bus to pick up North, 9, Penelope, 10, and several of their best buds, including Selena Gomez’s younger sister, Gracie Teefey. By the time the gig started, the Hulu personality — who shares North and three younger children with ex-husband Kanye West — was also in awe of the production.

“Such a good show! We love you @katyperry,” Kardashian gushed on Saturday, sharing concert videos of Perry, 38, singing “Dark Horse” atop a giant rocking horse. “I’m the biggest @katyperry fan!”

The American Idol judge later saw North grooving with her friends and invited the elementary schoolers to join her on The Theatre of Resorts World Las Vegas stage because she had seen “all of her TikToks.” North screeched about the opportunity to participate in Perry’s show dance-off.

“Can my friends come too?” the eldest child of Kardashian and West, 45, asked Perry, before her friends — sans cousin Penelope, who is the daughter of Kourtney Kardashian and ex Scott Disick — ran up to the stage. “[I want to be] everything [when I grow up].”

North concluded the dance break with an impressive cartwheel before getting Perry’s autograph backstage alongside her mother.

“Honestly, I’m the biggest Katy Perry fan,” Kim gushed in another Saturday video, posing with the “Roar” singer after the show. “I’m not just saying that because you’re here, like, because I don’t associate you with what’s onstage. It was the memories … Katy, we already have the same initial!”

Perry, for her part, was equally in awe of meeting the Selfish author, as well as fellow concert attendees Paris Hilton and Sia. “If there were two words to describe this show, Play, what would they be?” she asked Hilton, 42, before the heiress busted out her signature “That’s hot” compliment.
