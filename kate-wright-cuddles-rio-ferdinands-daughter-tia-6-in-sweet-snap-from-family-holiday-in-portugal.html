Kate Wright cuddles Rio Ferdinand's daughter Tia, six, in sweet snap from family holiday in Portugal

KATE Wright has shared a picture of herself cuddling Rio Ferdinand's daughter Tia in a sweet snap from their family holiday in Portugal.

The former Towie star has been enjoying a sun-soaked break with her boyfriend and his relatives - and she has been growing close to his kids.

Kate, 26, uploaded a snap to Instagram showing her cradling little Tia, six, in her arms during a night out with Rio's family.

The TV beauty is standing with a group of Rio's female family members and she captioned the shot: "The girlies & one sleepy."

The little girl is holding on to Kate and has her face turned away from the camera.

Rio has three kids from his marriage to wife Rebecca, who died of cancer in 2015.

The couple have been relaxing on a family holiday, and Kate has been seen posing for pictures in a bikini with Rio's younger sister Sian.

Sian also shared pictures of herself with Kate and other family members, and in one shot she calls them all "sisters".

In another caption, she credited Rio for being strong following the recent death of their mum.

She wrote: "Through the smiles the laughters the tears & hard moments you've been there each step of the way with me.

"It's been a long road and we both know it's not been easy, but thank you for being a rock when I've needed you, mummy would be so proud of the man you are today.

"I've got you always! Love you Big Bro..."

Rio recently took the next step in his romance with Kate by sharing his first picture of them together.

The snap, which was uploaded to Instagram, shows Rio and Kate posing by the pool with his friends and family members.
