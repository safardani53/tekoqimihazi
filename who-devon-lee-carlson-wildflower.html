Who is Devon Lee Carlson?

DEVON Lee Carlson is making headlines after she was named-checked in Charli XCX's lyrics for her Barbie song.

Here's everything you need to know about model, influencer and Wildflower Cases founder Devon Lee Carlson.

Who is Devon Lee Carlson?

Devon Lee Carlson, 28, is a fashion influencer and model branded "the Internet's It Girl".

She rocketed to fame after a chance encounter with Miley Cyrus - who took to Twitter with a snap of Devon's handmade phone case.

The publicity allowed Devon to launch her Wildflower Cases brand, now a staple of the LA fashion set.

Devon, whose style is influenced by Tumblr, Hannah Montana and Barbie, has designed t-shirts for Marc Jacobs and sat front row for Saint Laurent and Gucci.

Her cases have been sported by the likes of Dua Lipa and Trixie Mattel - and were also featured on teen drama Euphoria.

She is also known for her YouTube channel, where she posts reports from fashion shows in London, Paris, Milan and New York.

The influencer told Wonderland magazine that her fashion week essentials are tea, vodka, croissants and a good attitude.

Devon is reportedly dating actor Duke Nicholson, 24, the son of The Shining star Jack Nicholson.

The pair have been spotted together at Paris Fashion Week and the GQ Men of the Year Party in Hollywood - with Devon posting pics of Duke on Instagram, where she has 1.5million followers.

She also dated Jesse Rutherford, the lead singer of The Neighbourhood, before they broke up in 2021.

Devon appeared in the music videos for The Neighbourhood's songs "Pretty Boy", "Stargazing" and "Lost in Translation".

What is Devon Lee Carlson's net worth?

Devon Lee Carlson's net worth has been estimated at $1.5million.

Modelling work and designs for the likes of Burberry and Louis Vuitton have made Devon one of the fashion world's top earners.

Will Devon Lee Carlson appear in Barbie The Movie?

On June 1 2023 Devon Lee Carlson revealed that Charli XCX name-checks her in a song for the Barbie movie soundtrack.

Devon took to Instagram with a screenshot of a series of texts Charlie sent her.

They read: "Just wanted to let you know. I've done a song for the Barbie soundtrack and I name check you in it.

"Lyrics are: [Redacted] Devon Lee smile [Redacted] Van Gogh.

"Just wanted to tell you!"

Devon said: "You smile, I smile."

There has not been any suggestion to date that Devon will actually appear in Barbie The Movie.
