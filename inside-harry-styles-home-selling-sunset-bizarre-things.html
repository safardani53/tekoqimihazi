Inside Harry Styles' 6m Hollywood home as Selling Sunset stars reveal bizarre things he left behin

ONE Direction star Harry Styles’ incredible home has been shown off on Selling Sunset – and it’s a pad to marvel.

The 29-year-old is amongst the wealthiest under 35s in the UK, The Times Rich List recently revealed.

The One Direction sensation is worth an estimated £150m, making it no surprise he has a luxurious home.

Harry’s Los Angeles home was shown off as Selling Sunset stars Chrishell Stause and Emma Hernan went along to view it after it was put on the market for a cool $8million (£6million).

But now the Watermelon Sugar star is selling the home in St. Ives Place, after initially buying it in 2017.

The stunning 11,000-square-foot mansion is on the iconic Sunset Strip, and sits behind private gates.

And Harry’s lavish former home boasts an office, gym, home theatre and two-car garage, as well as a swimming pool and lush greenery as its surroundings.

As Chrishell and Emma toured around the home, they revealed the surprising items the singing sensation had left behind.

“He left a lot, like, hundreds of pairs of shoes. Like, heels,” Emma told Chrishell, adding: “I think they're gone now.”

Chrishell went on to joke: “We could say, "Harry, we have some shoes we need to return to you,” saying it was their way to get backstage at one of the shows.

The bedroom comes with incredible views, leading Emma to quip: “'Harry probably had some watermelon sugar in this bed right here.”

There are four bedrooms in total, including Harry’s, and six bathrooms, with the girls coining it as the “most impressive, beautiful home in the Hills.”

Harry released his third solo album Harry's House last year, which became the UK's biggest-selling record of 2022, shifting 460,000 units.

As a result of that and his many other successes, Harry has landed himself a place on the rich list.

It comes after fans were given some very exciting One Direction news over the weekend.

Liam Payne headed to the studio with his former 1D bandmates Niall Horan and Louis Tomlinson in Los Angeles, where he has been working on his eagerly awaited second album.

This means a mini One Direction reunion is on the way – just over seven years after the boys put the band on ice.

An insider said: “Liam is great pals with Niall and Louis, so loved catching up with them while they were all in LA. Being in the studio together felt natural, so they all jumped on a track.
