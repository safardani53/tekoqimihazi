Oaklands College: Police rush to school after 'incident' outside St Albans campus

POLICE have rushed to a school after an "incident" outside the campus.

It is understood armed officers were sent to Oaklands College in St Albans, Hertfordshire, Monday afternoon.

An air ambulance and multiple cops were also called to the scene on Monday.

A helicopter was also circulating the area as pupils tried leaving for the day.

It is not yet clear what has prompted the huge emergency response.

Oaklands College said: "We are aware of an incident that has occurred outside our St Albans campus today.
