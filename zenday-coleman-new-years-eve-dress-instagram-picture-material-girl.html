Zenday Colemans New Years Eve Dress SHOP Her Sparkling Mini Hollywood Life

Zenday Coleman’s New Year’s Eve Dress — SHOP Her Party Frock:

Zendaya brought in 2015 in the comfort of her own home, (and the comfort of her fluffy pink socks!), but that didn’t stop her from adding some glamour to her look in a chic mini dress. She proudly flaunted her Material Girl Notch Neckline Metallic Dress, and it’s available now for only 24.99.

The face of the fun junior’s line captioned the pic, “Wanted to still look fabulous on New Years *cough* at home *cough* so I rocked my @materialgirl dress…with my fuzzy socks.”

Even though we would’ve loved to see what fun shoes Zendaya would’ve paired with her Material Girl mini dress, she looked effortlessly haute in the metallic piece. After all, she does have a covetable shoe collection!

Zendaya Coleman Shows Off Her Versatile Style

Sometimes Zendaya flaunts her high fashion looks, but other time she shows off her tomboy side in trendy kicks.

She did just that on New Year’s Eve because even though she looked great in her sleeveless, skin baring look, she decided to trade it in for Jordan sneakers and cozy sweats.

We love her fun party dress, but this look was definitely more befitting of an evening inside with family. What do you think of her metallic Material Girl mini dress?
