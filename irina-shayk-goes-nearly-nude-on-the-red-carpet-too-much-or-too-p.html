Too Much or Too Perfect?

Last night’s Academy Awards offered the usual slate of fashion hits and misses, but the most memorable get-up of the night belonged to someone who wasn’t invited to the ceremony, and has no real connection to the movie industry.

That’s Irina Shayk at the Vanity Fair after-party, and yeah, the girl definitely knows how to turn a head or two.

Shayk split from Cristiano Ronaldo last month after five years of dating, and she clearly knows how to take advantage of the single life.

Of course, Irina Shayk and nudity go together like Oscar after-parties and desperate bids for attention, but usually, the Russian beauty strips down for private photo shoots, not public red carpet events.

It’s important to note, however, that the VF soiree was crawling with rich, good-looking single dudes, including Jared Leto, Orlando Bloom and Common.

Irina is 29, which makes her approximately 4,000 in model years. She’s back on the market for the first time in a long time, and she’s about a decade too old for Leonardo DiCaprio.

She’s pulling out all the stops, and we applaud her for it. We don’t care who went home with the statuettes; clearly, Irina Shayk was the real winner of the night.
