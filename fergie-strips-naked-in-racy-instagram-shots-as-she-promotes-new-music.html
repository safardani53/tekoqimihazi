Fergie strips naked in racy Instagram shots as she promotes new music

SHE is gearing up to release some new music and Fergie knows exactly how to get everyone talking about it – by stripping naked.

The 42-year-old treated fans to a black and white image of herself completely in the buff while lounging about.

Showing off her amazing figure, she wears just a pair of heels in the sultry shot, which sees her seductively looking over her shoulder.

The mum of one liked the picture so much she shared it twice, captioning one ‘tick’ and the other ‘tock’.

She has shared a series of images to tease her upcoming new song, giving a little away each time alongside a number of arty black and white images.

Her various captions  include ‘Exposed… but not fully’, ‘I see you’, ‘SPLIT’, ‘PERSONALITIES’, ‘been hearing voices’, ‘… and seeing double’, ‘knock knock’, ‘who’s there’ and ‘less chatter’.

It’s thought Fergie will be dropping her solo record Double Dutchess next month, after recently launching her own record label  - Dutchess Music.

But the singer was dealt a blow  last month when the album was leaked on the Internet.

Meanwhile, a spokesman for the star recently denied she had split from the Black Eyed Peas.

They said: “Fergie is still in the Black Eyed Peas and has an enormous amount of love for her brothers.
