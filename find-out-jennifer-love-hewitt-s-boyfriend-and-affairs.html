Find out Jennifer Love Hewitts Boyfriend and affairs

Actress Jennifer Love Hewitt has quite a name when it comes to her long list of ex-boyfriends. When the news of Hewitt’s wedding came out in 2013, a lot of fans became happy to know that she found her prince charming. Well, her marriage of 2013 is still going strong as there are no split rumors.

Hewitt is not just an actress, but also a singer, writer, producer, and director. She tied the knot with her beau Brian Hallisay. Although her current married life is going well, her love life appears a little bumpy until she got married.

Find out more about their love affairs and relationships here in FamousStardom.

Jennifer Love Hewitt first meeting with Brian Hallisay and marriage

Hewitt met her spouse, Hallisay, for the first time in March 2012 on the sets of ‘The Client List.' They began dating soon after that. They took their love affair to another level after they got engaged in June 2013.

Their engagement news didn’t come alone as it also had Hewitt's pregnancy news attached to it. Just before giving birth to their daughter Autumn, who was born in November, they exchanged the vows in late 2013. 

The adorable pair announced that they were expecting their second child in January 2015. Eventually, in June, Hewitt gave birth to an angelic son. They named him Atticus.

Jennifer Love Hewitt long list of boyfriends

Before the arrival of Hallisay in her life, Hewitt had several love affairs. The list of actors she dated is quite long. From 1995 to 1996, her name was linked with Joey Lawrence.

After that, she dated two stars the following year, Will Friedle and Andrew Keegan. Besides, she also dated Carson Daly, Jason, Aaron Baca, Diego Serrano, Rich Cronin, Enrique Iglesias, Patrick Wilson, Ethan Zohn, John Mayer.

The list doesn’t end just there. Her other relationships include Kip Pardue, Antonio Sabato Jr., Scott Austin, Jamie Kennedy, Alex Beh and Jarod Einsohn.

Jennifer Love Hewitt was engaged to Ross McCall

She was also engaged to Ross McCall in 2007. However, the engagement couldn’t last more than a year. They separated in 2008, but the reason behind their split wasn’t revealed.

Hewitt had many boyfriends in the past from 1995 to 2012 until she finally found the love of her life in 2012, Brian Hallisay. It's similar to what people say; love finds its way. We hope Hewitt’s relationship with her current husband won’t turn out like her past affairs. Since they have no divorce hints till date, we hope they won’t have any in the coming days as well.
