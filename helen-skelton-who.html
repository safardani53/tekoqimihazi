Who is Helen Skelton and why has she left BBC Radio 5? The Sun

HELEN Skelton has announced that she will be leaving BBC Radio 5 to spend more time with her kids.

The 40-year-old will step down from her Sunday morning slot after a year, admitting that she had found it tough juggling her job with being a single mum to her three children.

Who is Helen Skelton?

Helen Skelton was born on July 19, 1983, in Carlisle, Cumbria.

She grew up on a dairy farm in the village of Kirkby Thore before getting a BA in journalism from the Cumbria Institute of the Arts.

She is a qualified tap dance teacher and has said that if she was not a broadcaster, she would be a teacher.

Skelton began her career as an extra on Coronation Street and Cutting It.

She also co-presented two series of Holiday Hit Squad alongside Angela Rippon and Joe Crowley.

Which TV shows has Helen Skelton been on?

Helen is best known for presenting children's programmes Newsround and Blue Peter.

In 2008, she replaced Zoe Salmon on Blue Peter becoming the 33rd presenter for the programme.

She announced her departure from the show in August 2013 and was replaced by Radzi Chinyanganya.

After she left the children's magazine programme, she became a presenter of live FA Women's Super League football matches for the BT Sport channel.

She also presented two series of Holiday Hit Squad (2013-2014) and the London Marathon Highlights (2014-2015) show on the BBC.

In 2012, the star took part in a Christmas special edition of Strictly Come Dancing.

Since 2015, she has co-presented the daytime BBC One series The Instant Gardener alongside Danny Clarke.

Skelton hosted coverage of the 2016 European Swimming Championships and worked for the BBC at the 2016 Summer Olympics.

In October that year, Skelton guest presented five editions of Lorraine on ITV.

In 2018, she appeared in Celebrity Antiques Road Trip with her ex-co-presenter Rebecca Adlington.

Helen has also been a presenter on Countryfile and reported on rugby for Channel 4.

She also hosts Winter on the Farm on Channel 5.

In 2022, Helen landed the job of Radio 5 Live presenter following the departure of Laura Whitmore and competed on Strictly with professional dancer Gorka Marquez.

On August 13, 2023, Helen announced she would be leaving BBC Radio 5 to spend more time with her three kids.

Speaking to her co-host Lloyd Griffith, she said: "You're emotional because I'm leaving."

"I am," he replied, before asking how she was feeling.

Helen replied: "I am not all right about it but, you know, needs must. The juggle is real."

Is Helen Skelton married and does she have children?

Helen married England national rugby league team player Richie Myler in 2013.

Two years later, in June 2015, they had their first child together.

That same year, the couple moved to France for a year when Richie joined the Catalans Dragons.

They welcomed their second son in April 2017, and their first daughter in December 2021.

However, in April 2022 the couple revealed they had split up.

In December 2022, Helen opened up to The Telegraph about the breakdown of her marriage and her decision to do Strictly.

She said:"I don’t see myself as a victim. There are a lot of people who have been in my situation who haven’t been given the opportunities that I've got.

"Don’t get me wrong, I have my days when I'm p***** off. But equally, in this job, you're in a lot of people's lives, which gives you a phenomenal amount of perspective.

"I think mine is a job that gives you lots of perspective, so it's very difficult to say 'woe is me'."

In July 2023, it had been claimed by OK! that Helen is "reluctantly dipping her toe back into the dating pool".
