Who Is Bhad Bhabie Dating? Meet Boyfriend Le Vaughn Hollywood Life

Bhad Bhabie is going to have a baby of her own! The rapper, 20, revealed that she’s pregnant with her first child in an Instagram post on Friday, December 1. Bhad Bhabie, whose real name is Danielle Bregoli has been in a relationship with Le Vaughn for some time, and an insider confirmed that he’s the father to People. 

Prior to making the big announcement herself, there were some rumors that Bhad Bhabie was pregnant. She was spotted grabbing dinner at Porta Via in Beverly Hills with her beau just the day before sharing the news, per XXL.   Following the exciting news about Danielle’s pregnancy, get to know more about Le Vaughn, and some of the controversial social media personality‘s past romances here!

Bhad Bhabie & Le Vaughn Have Been Together Since At Least 2021

It’s not entirely clear when Danielle started dating Le Vaughn, who has over 40,000 Instagram followers. While the rapper has cleared up her Instagram, only one photo of them together has remained. She did reportedly post a now-deleted video with Le Vaughn back in 2021, per AmoMama. “Love this man 100x over,” she wrote in one of her deleted posts.

Prior to announcing her pregnancy, Bhad Bhabie did share a cute photo of the two of them sitting courtside at an NBA game. Le Vaughn’s hand was resting on her leg. In the caption, she wrote, “Happy Birthday baby” with a light blue heart emoji.

Bhad Bhabie Has a Tattoo for Her Beau

Bhad Bhabie is no stranger to tattoos, and she even appeared to have one that made reference to her beau. She revealed that she got a tattoo that had the time “11:11” written out in April 2023, per TMZ. While that is certainly a lucky time for many people, it’s also the same as Le Vaughn’s birthday: November 11 (11/11).

Bhad Bhabie Has Made Appearances on Le Vaughn’s Social Media

While the pair appear to mostly keep their romance private, Le Vaughn has posted a handful of photos of himself with his girlfriend over the year. He also posted a courtside photo on his birthday, as well as Danielle adjusting her shoes when he showed off some of his collection. In another photo, the pair appeared to be backstage at a concert.

Le Vaughn Is Already a Father

Danielle’s baby isn’t going to be Le Vaughn’s first child. He’s already a father to one baby boy. He posted a few cute photos of his son back in July 2023, and he wished his son a happy birthday on Instagram.

Bhad Bhabie’s Past Relationships

While Danielle has mostly kept her relationships private, she did appear to reveal that she was briefly dating fellow rapper Yung Bans when she posted a photo of the two of them in bed together. As she was under 18 at the time, fans were concerned, because Yung Bans was 20. She defended him in an Instagram comment, via VladTV. “He’s 20 i’m 17…ain’t nothing wrong with 3 years so relax,” she wrote.
